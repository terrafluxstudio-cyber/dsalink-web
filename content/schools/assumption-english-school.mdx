Assumption English School — commonly known as AES — is a government-aided Catholic co-educational school located on Upper Bukit Timah Road in the Bukit Panjang area of north-west Singapore. Part of the St Gabriel's Foundation family of schools, it combines the national curriculum with values-based character development.

Assumption English offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its strong debating and public-speaking culture, its judo and team-sports tradition, and an active student leadership programme.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Assumption English offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Assumption English is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Assumption English official website](https://www.assumptionenglish.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning (Communication) and Leadership

Assumption English runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) focused on speaking English with confidence and knowledge, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on community youth leadership. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Assumption English co-educational?

Yes. Assumption English School is a co-educational school.

### Does Assumption English offer IP?

No. Assumption English offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Assumption English?

Assumption English is competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Assumption English offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Assumption English participates in DSA-Sec across sports, academic, and leadership talent areas, including a distinctive judo and debating tradition. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Assumption English?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Assumption English if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
