St. Margaret's School (Secondary) — commonly known as St. Margaret's or SMSS — is a government-aided, autonomous, Anglican school for girls located on Farrer Road in the Bukit Timah area of central Singapore. Founded in 1842, it is recognised as the oldest girls' school in Singapore. As an autonomous school with an Anglican mission heritage, St. Margaret's combines the national curriculum with values-based character development.

St. Margaret's offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is distinctive for offering the Enhanced Music Programme (EMP), an MOE-designated music pathway, and is known for its broad performing arts culture and an active sports tradition.

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

St. Margaret's offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: St. Margaret's is a mainstream autonomous school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [St. Margaret's official website](https://www.stmargaretssec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Enhanced Music Programme (EMP)

St. Margaret's offers the Enhanced Music Programme (EMP), an MOE-designated upper-secondary music programme that allows students to study music at a deeper level as an O-Level subject, with access to a Music Centre. The EMP is offered at a small number of secondary schools and is a recognised talent pathway for students with strong musical ability.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Admission Pathways

### PSLE and S1 Posting

St. Margaret's admits students through the S1 posting exercise across its Express and Normal posting bands. As an affiliated school, students from St. Margaret's School (Primary) may receive affiliation priority in posting, subject to MOE's rules. The cut-off point (COP) differs by track and by affiliation status, and changes each year. For current and historical PSLE COP data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### DSA-Sec Admission

St. Margaret's participates in DSA-Sec across music, performing arts, sports, and academic talent areas. As a girls' school with the Enhanced Music Programme, a broad multicultural dance and band culture, and distinctive sports such as fencing alongside netball, bowling, and tennis, St. Margaret's DSA tends to attract applicants across these strengths. DSA offers place students into the school's mainstream tracks.

The specific talent areas offered change from year to year. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) for the current year's full list. For key application dates, see the [DSA-Sec Timeline](/timeline). For preparation by talent area, see [DSA Interview Preparation](/dsa-interview). For historical results context, see [DSA Results](/dsa-results).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is St. Margaret's only for girls?

Yes. St. Margaret's School (Secondary) is a single-sex school for girls, and is recognised as the oldest girls' school in Singapore.

### Does St. Margaret's offer IP?

No. St. Margaret's offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What is the Enhanced Music Programme at St. Margaret's?

The Enhanced Music Programme (EMP) is an MOE-designated upper-secondary programme that lets students study music at a deeper level as an O-Level subject, with access to a Music Centre. A music talent pathway may also be available through DSA-Sec; check the [St. Margaret's DSA admissions page](https://www.stmargaretssec.moe.edu.sg/admissions/) and the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) for the current year's confirmed talent areas.

### What PSLE score does my daughter need for St. Margaret's?

St. Margaret's is an autonomous school competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and affiliation status and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does St. Margaret's offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. St. Margaret's participates in DSA-Sec across music, performing arts, sports, and academic talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my daughter is not offered a DSA place at St. Margaret's?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to St. Margaret's if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices, with affiliation priority applying where relevant. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
