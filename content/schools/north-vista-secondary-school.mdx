North Vista Secondary School — commonly known as NVSS — is a government co-educational school located on Rivervale Link in the Sengkang area of north-eastern Singapore. The school combines the national curriculum with values-based character development and applied-learning emphases in STEM and the arts.

North Vista offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its golf and team-sports tradition, its band and string-ensemble culture, and a visual arts and design focus.

---

## What makes North Vista distinctive

North Vista's identity blends visual arts and design with a genuinely uncommon DSA strength: golf. Its Applied Learning Programme runs in STEM with a cities and urban-landscapes angle, while its Learning for Life Programme centres on visual arts and design — so creativity is built into the school's everyday culture, not bolted on. For DSA-Sec families, the golf and team-sports tradition stands out, since few neighbourhood schools offer a clear competitive-golf pathway, and the band and string-ensemble culture gives musicians a strong home alongside the design focus. Sitting in Sengkang, it serves north-eastern families as a mainstream Express and Normal school. A child whose talent is visual, musical, or in a sport like golf that not every school supports will find North Vista an unusually good match. Confirm this year's talent areas in the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder), and see the [DSA-Sec Timeline](/timeline) for application dates.

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

North Vista offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: North Vista is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [North Vista official website](https://www.northvistasec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning, STEM and the Arts

North Vista runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in STEM with a cities and urban-landscapes focus, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on visual arts and design. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is North Vista Secondary co-educational?

Yes. North Vista Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does North Vista offer IP?

No. North Vista offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for North Vista?

North Vista is competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does North Vista offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. North Vista participates in DSA-Sec across sports, performing arts, and academic talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at North Vista?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to North Vista if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
