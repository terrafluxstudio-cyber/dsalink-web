Temasek Secondary School — commonly known as TMS — is a government autonomous co-educational school located on Upper East Coast Road in eastern Singapore. As an autonomous school, Temasek has greater flexibility over curriculum enrichment and co-curricular programming than standard government schools, within MOE's national framework.

Temasek offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its performing arts and leadership culture, its shooting and dance programmes, and an active student development tradition.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Temasek offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Temasek is a mainstream autonomous school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Temasek official website](https://www.temaseksec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Student Development and Leadership

Temasek places a strong emphasis on student leadership and holistic development alongside its academic curriculum, reflected in its leadership talent pathway and an active co-curricular culture. For current programme details, refer to the official website.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Campus and Facilities

Temasek Secondary School is located at **600 Upper East Coast Road, Singapore 465561** in the Upper East Coast / Bedok South area of eastern Singapore.

**Nearest MRT:**
- **Bedok** (East West Line EW5) — connected to the school by feeder bus services (approximately a 10-minute ride)
- **Tanah Merah** (East West Line EW4) — a short bus ride away

The Upper East Coast stretch is not within direct walking distance of an MRT station, so buses are the common way to reach the school. Campus facilities include general and specialist classrooms, science laboratories, a multi-purpose hall, performing arts and music spaces, and sports courts. For current facility details and a campus map, refer to the [Temasek official website](https://www.temaseksec.moe.edu.sg/).

## Admission Pathways

### PSLE and S1 Posting

Temasek admits students through the S1 posting exercise across its Express and Normal posting bands. The cut-off point (COP) differs by track and changes each year. For current and historical PSLE COP data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Temasek Secondary co-educational?

Yes. Temasek Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Temasek offer IP?

No. Temasek offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Temasek?

Temasek is an autonomous school competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### How do students get to Temasek Secondary?

The school is on Upper East Coast Road, which is not within direct walking distance of an MRT station. Most students travel by bus, with feeder services connecting from Bedok and Tanah Merah MRT stations. Check the [Temasek official website](https://www.temaseksec.moe.edu.sg/) for current transport details.

### Does Temasek offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Temasek participates in DSA-Sec across performing arts, sports, and leadership talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Temasek?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Temasek if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
