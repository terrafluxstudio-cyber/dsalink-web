Nan Hua High School — commonly abbreviated NHHS — is a government autonomous SAP (Special Assistance Plan) secondary school located in Clementi in western Singapore. Founded in 1917, it is one of Singapore's oldest schools and the only SAP school directly under the government (as opposed to government-aided). NHHS is designated by MOE as a West Zone Centre of Excellence for Chinese Language and Culture.

NHHS offers the O-Level Express track. Unlike some other SAP schools, NHHS does not offer an Integrated Programme — all students sit for the GCE O-Level examinations at the end of Secondary 4. The school's distinctive identity centres on its SAP Flagship Programme in Chinese Language and Culture, its long tradition of performing arts excellence, and its bilingual, bicultural school environment.

## Programmes and Curriculum

### O-Level Express (4-year)

NHHS admits all students into the four-year O-Level Express track. Students sit the Singapore-Cambridge O-Level examinations at the end of Secondary 4. As a SAP school, Chinese is offered at a high level alongside English, with the expectation that students maintain proficiency in both languages.

For P6 families: NHHS does not offer an IP track. Students who prefer to avoid O-Level examinations would need to consider other SAP schools that offer IP, such as Dunman High or River Valley High. Refer to the [NHHS official website](https://www.nanhuahigh.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### SAP Flagship Programme (Chinese Language and Culture)

As a SAP school and West Zone Centre of Excellence for Chinese Language and Culture, NHHS offers a flagship programme in Chinese language education and cultural studies. The school integrates Chinese cultural exposure, bilingual enrichment, and a range of Chinese language talent pathways into its mainstream O-Level curriculum.

### Malay Special Programme (MSP)

NHHS offers the Malay Special Programme (MSP), an MOE-designated programme for promising students who wish to develop exceptional Malay language proficiency alongside their mainstream studies. MSP is available at a limited number of secondary schools in Singapore.

### Other Programmes

NHHS also offers a Sustainable Environment Education Programme and participates in the national Digital Literacy Programme. For the full list of current programmes, refer to the [NHHS official website](https://www.nanhuahigh.moe.edu.sg/).

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Admission Pathways

### PSLE and S1 Posting

NHHS admits students through the S1 posting exercise. As a SAP school, it is listed under the SAP category in the posting process, and the COP reflects the school's competitive position for the O-Level Express posting band. For current and historical PSLE COP data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### DSA-Sec Admission

NHHS participates in DSA-Sec with talent areas spanning Performing Arts, Sports, Language, and Leadership. DSA talent areas at NHHS include:

**Performing Arts:** Chinese Drama, Chinese Orchestra, Chinese and Modern Dance, Choir, Concert Band, English Drama, Guzheng Ensemble, Harp Ensemble  
**Visual Arts:** Chinese Calligraphy  
**Sports:** Badminton, Basketball, Netball, Softball, Table Tennis, Cross Country  
**Language & Humanities:** Debating  
**Leadership:** School leadership roles

All DSA-Sec offers at NHHS are into the O-Level Express track.

Talent areas offered change from year to year. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) for the current year's full list. For key application dates, see the [DSA-Sec Timeline](/timeline). ## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Nan Hua High School co-educational?

Yes. NHHS is a co-educational school.

### Does NHHS offer IP or only O-Level?

NHHS offers only the O-Level Express track. All students sit the GCE O-Level examinations at the end of Secondary 4. NHHS does not offer an Integrated Programme. Families seeking a SAP school with an IP track should consider Dunman High School or River Valley High School.

### What PSLE score does my child need for NHHS?

NHHS is a SAP school with an autonomous classification, and the COP changes each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Is NHHS a SAP school and what does that mean?

Yes. NHHS is a SAP (Special Assistance Plan) school. SAP schools offer Chinese as the first language alongside English, with a strong emphasis on bilingual and Chinese cultural development. Students at NHHS are expected to have good Chinese language proficiency. The SAP environment is designed for students who want a bicultural education grounded in both English and Chinese.

### Does NHHS offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. NHHS participates in DSA-Sec across Performing Arts, Sports, Visual Arts, Language, and Leadership talent areas. All DSA offers are into the O-Level Express track. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's full list.

### What is the Harp Ensemble and is it available through DSA?

The Harp Ensemble at NHHS is one of the more distinctive CCAs offered in Singapore secondary schools. It may be listed as a DSA-Sec talent area. Check the [NHHS DSA admissions page](https://www.nanhuahigh.moe.edu.sg/our-experience/programmes/direct-school-admission-dsa/) and the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) for the current year's confirmed DSA talent areas, as offerings change year to year.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at NHHS?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to NHHS if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank NHHS in their school choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
