Assumption Pathway School (APS) is a government-aided co-educational pathway school located in the Bishan area of central Singapore. Operating under the auspices of the Catholic Church, the school provides an alternative secondary education programme for students who benefit from a more structured, values-based, and supportive learning environment.

Assumption Pathway School does not follow the mainstream secondary Express or Normal academic tracks. Students follow a customised pathway programme anchored in values education and holistic development, building academic foundations and life skills alongside character and community. APS does not currently offer DSA-Sec.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Pathway Programme

Assumption Pathway School offers a customised pathway curriculum rather than the mainstream O-Level or N-Level tracks. The programme integrates academic learning with values education, life skills, and character development, providing a structured and supportive environment for students who need an alternative educational approach.

For P6 families: APS is a pathway school, not a mainstream secondary school. Admission is through MOE referral rather than standard PSLE COP and S1 posting. Refer to the [APS official website](https://www.aps.moe.edu.sg/) for the current programme structure and entry criteria.

### Values Education and Character Development

APS's mission school heritage shapes its emphasis on values, character, and community. The school's programme includes structured values education alongside academic and applied learning, developing students as grounded individuals with purpose and direction.

For context on the secondary school system, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is Assumption Pathway School?

Assumption Pathway School is a government-aided pathway school that provides an alternative secondary education for students who benefit from a more structured, values-based learning environment. Its mission school heritage places values, character, and community at the centre of the school experience.

### Is APS a government school?

APS is a government-aided school, which means it receives government funding and operates under MOE guidelines, while being managed by the Catholic Church. This is similar to other mission and religious schools in Singapore.

### How is admission to APS different from standard S1 posting?

Admission to APS is through MOE referral rather than PSLE COP and standard S1 posting. Refer to the [APS official website](https://www.aps.moe.edu.sg/) for current entry criteria.

### Does APS offer DSA-Sec?

APS does not currently offer DSA-Sec. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to confirm the latest status.

### What are the options after Assumption Pathway School?

APS prepares students for further education and employment pathways. Refer to the [APS official website](https://www.aps.moe.edu.sg/) for post-secondary progression information.
