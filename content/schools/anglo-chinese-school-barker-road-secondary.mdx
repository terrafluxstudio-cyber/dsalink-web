Anglo-Chinese School (Barker Road) — commonly known as ACS (Barker Road) or ACSBR — is a government-aided Methodist boys' school located on Barker Road in the Newton / Novena area of central Singapore. It is part of the Anglo-Chinese Schools family, which traces its founding to 1886, and shares the ACS motto and Methodist heritage.

ACS (Barker Road) offers the mainstream secondary tracks: the Express (O-Level) track, the Normal (Academic) track, and the Normal (Technical) track, with full Subject-Based Banding. Unlike ACS (Independent), ACS (Barker Road) does not offer the IP or IB; it follows the national O-Level and N-Level pathways. The school is affiliated to the broader ACS family of schools, including ACS (Primary) and ACS (Junior), which is relevant for affiliation priority in posting.

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express, Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical)

ACS (Barker Road) offers all three mainstream secondary courses. Express students prepare for the GCE O-Level examinations over four years; Normal (Academic) and Normal (Technical) students follow the N-Level pathways with progression options into further study. The school implements full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: ACS (Barker Road) is a mainstream affiliated school rather than an IP school. Its track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [ACS (Barker Road) official website](https://www.acsbr.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied and Life-Readiness Programmes

The school offers an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) with a focus on language, communication, and public speaking, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on sports and outdoor education. These programmes are part of the school's broader character and life-readiness development within the ACS tradition.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Admission Pathways

### PSLE and S1 Posting

ACS (Barker Road) admits students through the S1 posting exercise across its Express and Normal posting bands. As an affiliated school within the ACS family, students from affiliated ACS primary schools may receive affiliation priority in posting, subject to MOE's posting rules. The cut-off point (COP) differs by track and by affiliation status, and changes each year. For current and historical PSLE COP data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### DSA-Sec Admission

ACS (Barker Road) participates in DSA-Sec across sports, performing arts, and academic talent areas. As a boys' school with a strong band, rugby, water polo, and football tradition, ACS (Barker Road)'s DSA tends to attract applicants across these sporting and performing arts strengths. ## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is ACS (Barker Road) only for boys?

Yes. ACS (Barker Road) is a single-sex school for boys.

### Does ACS (Barker Road) offer IP or IB?

No. ACS (Barker Road) offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. The IP and IB Diploma within the ACS family are offered at ACS (Independent), which is a separate school.

### What is the difference between ACS (Barker Road) and ACS (Independent)?

ACS (Barker Road) is a government-aided school offering the national Express and Normal tracks. ACS (Independent) is an independent school offering the Integrated Programme leading to the IB Diploma. They are separate schools with different admission processes, different campuses, and different DSA talent area offerings. For DSA-Sec purposes, apply to each separately and check each school's official DSA page.

### Does my son get priority if he comes from an ACS primary school?

ACS (Barker Road) is affiliated to ACS primary schools, and affiliation priority may apply in the S1 posting exercise subject to MOE's rules. For DSA-Sec, admission is governed by MOE's centralised DSA framework and talent-based selection, separate from affiliation priority. Check the [ACS (Barker Road) admissions page](https://www.acsbr.moe.edu.sg/admissions/) for current details.

### Does ACS (Barker Road) offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. ACS (Barker Road) participates in DSA-Sec across sports, performing arts, and academic talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my son is not offered a DSA place at ACS (Barker Road)?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to ACS (Barker Road) if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices, with affiliation priority applying where relevant. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
