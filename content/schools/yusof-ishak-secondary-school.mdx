Yusof Ishak Secondary School — commonly known as YISS — is a government co-educational school located on Sumang Walk in the Punggol area of north-eastern Singapore. The school combines the national curriculum with values-based character development and an applied-learning emphasis in STEM.

Yusof Ishak offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its fencing and wushu tradition, its robotics and STEM focus, and an active community leadership culture.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Yusof Ishak offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Yusof Ishak is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Yusof Ishak official website](https://www.yusofishaksec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning and Leadership

Yusof Ishak runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in STEM with a sustainability focus, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on community youth leadership. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Yusof Ishak Secondary co-educational?

Yes. Yusof Ishak Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Yusof Ishak offer IP?

No. Yusof Ishak offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Yusof Ishak?

Yusof Ishak is competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Yusof Ishak offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Yusof Ishak participates in DSA-Sec across sports, academic or applied-technology, and leadership talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Yusof Ishak?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Yusof Ishak if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
