Yishun Town Secondary School — commonly known as YTSS — is a government, autonomous, co-educational school located on Yishun Street 21 in the Yishun area of northern Singapore. As an autonomous school, Yishun Town has greater flexibility over curriculum enrichment and co-curricular programming than standard government schools, within MOE's national framework.

Yishun Town offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is distinctive for offering the Language Elective Programme (Tamil), an MOE-designated language pathway, and is known for its shooting and bowling traditions alongside an active performing arts and bilingual culture.

---

## What makes Yishun Town distinctive

Yishun Town Secondary is an autonomous school with a language-and-precision-sport identity. It is one of a small number of schools offering the Language Elective Programme (Tamil), an MOE-designated pathway, and is known for shooting and bowling traditions — precision sports few schools combine — alongside a performing-arts and bilingual culture. For DSA-Sec families, the draws include Tamil language and culture, competitive shooting and bowling, a Chinese dance culture, and team sports such as basketball and volleyball. The best fit is a child with a language-and-culture strength, a precision-sport talent, a performing-arts ability, or a team-sport strength. As an autonomous Express and Normal school in Yishun, a DSA offer places students into those tracks. Confirm this year's talent areas in the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder).

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Yishun Town offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Yishun Town is a mainstream autonomous school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Yishun Town official website](https://www.yishuntownsec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Language Elective Programme (Tamil)

Yishun Town offers the Language Elective Programme (Tamil) — an MOE-designated pathway for students with a strong interest and ability in Tamil language and literature. It is offered at only a small number of secondary schools. The school also emphasises bilingualism more broadly as part of its character and identity.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Campus and Facilities

Yishun Town Secondary School is located at **6 Yishun Street 21, Singapore 768610** in the Yishun area of northern Singapore.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Yishun Town Secondary co-educational?

Yes. Yishun Town Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Yishun Town offer IP?

No. Yishun Town offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What is the Language Elective Programme (Tamil) at Yishun Town?

It is an MOE-designated programme for students with strong ability and interest in Tamil language and literature, offered at only a few secondary schools. For current details, refer to the [Yishun Town official website](https://www.yishuntownsec.moe.edu.sg/).

### What PSLE score does my child need for Yishun Town?

Yishun Town is competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Yishun Town offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Yishun Town participates in DSA-Sec across sports, performing arts, and academic or language talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Yishun Town?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Yishun Town if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
