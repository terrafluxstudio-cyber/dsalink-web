Yio Chu Kang Secondary School — commonly known as YCKSS — is a government co-educational school located on Ang Mo Kio Avenue 5 in the Ang Mo Kio area of north-east Singapore. The school combines the national curriculum with values-based character development and an applied-learning emphasis in emerging technologies.

Yio Chu Kang offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its applied-learning focus in robotics and digital media, its multicultural dance and performing arts culture, and an active sports tradition.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Yio Chu Kang offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Yio Chu Kang is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Yio Chu Kang official website](https://www.yiochukangsec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning and the Arts

Yio Chu Kang runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in STEM with an emerging-technologies and technological-sustainability focus, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on character development through music, the performing arts, media and design. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Yio Chu Kang Secondary co-educational?

Yes. Yio Chu Kang Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Yio Chu Kang offer IP?

No. Yio Chu Kang offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Yio Chu Kang?

Yio Chu Kang is competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Yio Chu Kang offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Yio Chu Kang participates in DSA-Sec across sports, performing arts, and academic or applied-technology talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Yio Chu Kang?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Yio Chu Kang if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
