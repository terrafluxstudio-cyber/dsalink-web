Damai Secondary School — commonly known as DMSS — is a government co-educational school located on Bedok Reservoir Road in the Bedok area of eastern Singapore. The school combines the national curriculum with values-based character development and an applied-learning emphasis in environmental science.

Damai offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its canoeing and rugby tradition, its band and choral culture, and an active water and adventure sports focus — taking advantage of its location beside Bedok Reservoir.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Damai offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Damai is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Damai official website](https://www.damaisec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning and Outdoor Education

Damai runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in STEM with an environmental-science and sustainability focus, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on character development through water and adventure sports. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

Damai admits students through the S1 posting exercise across its Express and Normal posting bands. The cut-off point (COP) differs by track and changes each year. For current and historical PSLE COP data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Damai Secondary co-educational?

Yes. Damai Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Damai offer IP?

No. Damai offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Damai?

Damai is competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Damai offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Damai participates in DSA-Sec across sports, performing arts, and academic talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Damai?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Damai if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
