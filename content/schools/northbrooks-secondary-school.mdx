Northbrooks Secondary School — commonly known as NBKS — is a government co-educational school located on Yishun Ring Road in the Yishun area of northern Singapore. The school combines the national curriculum with values-based character development and an applied-learning emphasis in STEM and aerospace.

Northbrooks offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is distinctive for its aerospace and applied-technology focus, alongside a band and dance culture and a strong outdoor-adventure and sports tradition.

---

## What makes Northbrooks distinctive

Northbrooks has a distinctive aerospace-and-outdoors identity. Its Applied Learning Programme sits in STEM with a future-of-transportation and aerospace focus — an uncommon, specific hook for a child fascinated by flight — alongside a strong outdoor-adventure and sports tradition. Beyond that, the school carries a concert band and guzheng and dance culture. For DSA-Sec families, that draws a STEM-and-aerospace strength together with outdoor adventure and competitive floorball, basketball and badminton, plus musical talent. The best fit is a child with a real interest in aerospace or applied STEM, an outdoor-adventure strength, a musical talent, or a team-sport ability. Sitting in Yishun, Northbrooks serves northern families as a mainstream Express and Normal school, so a DSA offer places students into those tracks. Confirm this year's talent areas in the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder).

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Northbrooks offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Northbrooks is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Northbrooks official website](https://www.northbrooks.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning (STEM / Aerospace) and Outdoor Education

Northbrooks runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in STEM focused on the future of transportation, with an aerospace and applied-technology emphasis, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on sports and outdoor education. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Northbrooks Secondary co-educational?

Yes. Northbrooks Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Northbrooks offer IP?

No. Northbrooks offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Northbrooks?

Northbrooks is competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Northbrooks offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Northbrooks participates in DSA-Sec across sports, performing arts, and academic or applied-technology talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Northbrooks?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Northbrooks if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
