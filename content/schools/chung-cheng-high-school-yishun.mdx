Chung Cheng High School (Yishun) — commonly known as CCHY — is a government-aided co-educational school located on Yishun Street 61 in northern Singapore. Sharing the heritage of the Chung Cheng family of schools, it combines the national curriculum with values-based character development and a strong Chinese cultural tradition.

Chung Cheng High (Yishun) offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its strong Chinese orchestra and dance ensembles, its wushu and team-sports tradition, and an applied-learning focus in STEM.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Chung Cheng High (Yishun) offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Chung Cheng High (Yishun) is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Chung Cheng High (Yishun) official website](https://www.chungchenghighyishun.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning and the Arts

Chung Cheng High (Yishun) runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in STEM focused on health and food science, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on music and the performing arts. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Chung Cheng High (Yishun) co-educational?

Yes. Chung Cheng High School (Yishun) is a co-educational school.

### Does Chung Cheng High (Yishun) offer IP?

No. Chung Cheng High (Yishun) offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### Is Chung Cheng High (Yishun) the same as Chung Cheng High (Main)?

They share the Chung Cheng heritage but are separate schools on different campuses. Chung Cheng High (Yishun) is in Yishun and offers the Express, Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks; Chung Cheng High (Main) is in the Mountbatten area and is a SAP school. Admission to each is separate.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Chung Cheng High (Yishun)?

Chung Cheng High (Yishun) is competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Chung Cheng High (Yishun) offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Chung Cheng High (Yishun) participates in DSA-Sec across performing arts, sports, and academic talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Chung Cheng High (Yishun)?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Chung Cheng High (Yishun) if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
