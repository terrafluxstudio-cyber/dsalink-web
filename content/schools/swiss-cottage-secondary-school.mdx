Swiss Cottage Secondary School — commonly known as SCSS — is a government co-educational school located on Bukit Batok Street 34 in the west of Singapore. It offers the national curriculum with a focus on applied learning and student development.

Swiss Cottage offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its applied-learning focus in science and sustainability, its band and performing arts culture, and an active sports tradition.

---

## What makes Swiss Cottage distinctive

Swiss Cottage pairs a science-and-sustainability identity with one of the broader performing-arts cultures among western neighbourhood schools. Its Applied Learning Programme sits in STEM, with a sustainability and applied-sciences focus that runs through project work, while its Learning for Life Programme centres on community youth leadership. For DSA-Sec families, the appeal is range: the school carries a strong band, Chinese orchestra and dance culture alongside competitive bowling and floorball, so applicants in music, dance, a niche sport or a STEM-leaning profile all have somewhere to land. That breadth suits a child whose talent is real but who is not narrowly specialised — Swiss Cottage gives several directions room to grow rather than funnelling everyone toward one signature CCA. It is a mainstream Express and Normal school in Bukit Batok, well connected for west-side families. Confirm this year's talent areas in the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder), and see the [DSA-Sec Timeline](/timeline) for key dates.

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express, Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical)

Swiss Cottage offers all three mainstream secondary courses. Express students prepare for the GCE O-Level examinations over four years; Normal (Academic) and Normal (Technical) students follow the N-Level pathways with progression options. The school implements full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Swiss Cottage is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Swiss Cottage official website](https://www.swisscottagesec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning (STEM and Sustainability)

Swiss Cottage runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in STEM with a focus on sustainability and applied sciences, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on community youth leadership. These MOE programmes anchor the school's applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Swiss Cottage co-educational?

Yes. Swiss Cottage Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Swiss Cottage offer IP?

No. Swiss Cottage offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Swiss Cottage?

Swiss Cottage is competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Swiss Cottage offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Swiss Cottage participates in DSA-Sec across sports, performing arts, academic or STEM, and leadership talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Swiss Cottage?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Swiss Cottage if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
