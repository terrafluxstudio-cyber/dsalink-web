Bendemeer Secondary School — commonly known as BSS — is a government co-educational school located on St Wilfred Road in the Kallang area of central Singapore. The school combines the national curriculum with values-based character development and an applied-learning emphasis in STEM and sustainability.

Bendemeer offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its football and tchoukball tradition, its band and choral culture, and an active environmental and community leadership focus.

---

## What makes Bendemeer distinctive

Bendemeer has a sporting-and-sustainability identity. The school is known for a football and tchoukball tradition — tchoukball being a niche sport few schools offer — while its Applied Learning Programme sits in STEM with an environmental, eco-stewardship focus. For DSA-Sec families, those sports are the clearest draws, alongside a concert band and choral culture and an active environmental-leadership strand. The best fit is a child with a team-sport or niche-sport talent, a musical strength, or an environmental-and-leadership inclination, rather than a purely academic setting. Sitting in Kallang, Bendemeer serves central-area families as a mainstream Express and Normal school, so a DSA offer places students into those tracks. ## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Bendemeer offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Bendemeer is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Bendemeer official website](https://www.bendemeersec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning and Sustainability

Bendemeer runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in STEM with an emerging-technologies and eco-stewardship focus, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on community youth leadership. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Bendemeer Secondary co-educational?

Yes. Bendemeer Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Bendemeer offer IP?

No. Bendemeer offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Bendemeer?

Bendemeer is competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Bendemeer offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Bendemeer participates in DSA-Sec across sports, performing arts, and academic talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Bendemeer?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Bendemeer if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
