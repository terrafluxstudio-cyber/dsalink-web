Yuan Ching Secondary School is a government co-educational school located on Yuan Ching Road in the Jurong area of western Singapore. The school combines the national curriculum with values-based character development and an applied-learning emphasis in engineering innovation and community leadership.

Yuan Ching offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its Engineering Innovation and Solutions focus, its community youth leadership culture, and a technology-driven applied-learning approach that develops students for an innovation economy.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Yuan Ching offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Yuan Ching is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Yuan Ching official website](https://www.yuanchingsec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning and Engineering Innovation

Yuan Ching runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in Engineering Innovation and Solutions, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on community youth leadership. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Yuan Ching Secondary co-educational?

Yes. Yuan Ching Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Yuan Ching offer IP?

No. Yuan Ching offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Yuan Ching?

Yuan Ching's COP differs by track and changes each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Yuan Ching offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Yuan Ching participates in DSA-Sec across community youth leadership and Engineering Innovation and Solutions. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Yuan Ching?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Yuan Ching if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
