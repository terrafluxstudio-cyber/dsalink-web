Whitley Secondary School is a government co-educational school located on Whitley Road in the Bishan area of central Singapore. The school combines the national curriculum with values-based character development and an emphasis on communication, leadership, and student voice.

Whitley offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its debating and public speaking tradition, its leadership and character development focus, and a co-curricular culture that builds confident, articulate students.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Whitley offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Whitley is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Whitley official website](https://www.whitleysec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning and Leadership

Whitley runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in a communications or humanities focus, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on leadership and character development. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Whitley Secondary co-educational?

Yes. Whitley Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Whitley offer IP?

No. Whitley offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Whitley?

Whitley's COP differs by track and changes each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Whitley offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Whitley participates in DSA-Sec across debating and public speaking, and leadership and character. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Whitley?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Whitley if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
