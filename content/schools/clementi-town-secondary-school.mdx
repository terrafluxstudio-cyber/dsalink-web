Clementi Town Secondary School — commonly known as CTSS — is a government co-educational school located on Clementi Avenue 3 in the Clementi area of western Singapore. The school combines the national curriculum with values-based character development and an applied-learning emphasis in STEM.

Clementi Town offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its strong uniformed-group tradition, its band and performing arts culture, and a wushu and team-sports following.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Clementi Town offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Clementi Town is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Clementi Town official website](https://www.clementitownsec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning and the Arts

Clementi Town runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in STEM with an emerging-technologies focus, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on music and the performing arts. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Clementi Town Secondary co-educational?

Yes. Clementi Town Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Clementi Town offer IP?

No. Clementi Town offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Clementi Town?

Clementi Town is competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Clementi Town offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Clementi Town participates in DSA-Sec across uniformed-group, sports, and performing arts talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Clementi Town?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Clementi Town if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
