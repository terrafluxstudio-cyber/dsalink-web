Bukit View Secondary School — commonly known as BVSS — is a government co-educational school located on Bukit Batok Street 21 in the Bukit Batok area of western Singapore. The school combines the national curriculum with values-based character development and an applied-learning emphasis in STEM.

Bukit View offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its aerospace, aviation, and coding focus, its band and performing arts culture, and an active team-sports tradition.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Bukit View offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Bukit View is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Bukit View official website](https://www.bukitviewsec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning and the Arts

Bukit View runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in STEM with a clean-energy and environmental-technology focus, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on music and the performing arts. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Bukit View Secondary co-educational?

Yes. Bukit View Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Bukit View offer IP?

No. Bukit View offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Bukit View?

Bukit View is competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Bukit View offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Bukit View participates in DSA-Sec across sports, academic or applied-technology, and performing arts talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Bukit View?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Bukit View if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
