Springfield Secondary School is a government co-educational school located in the Choa Chu Kang area of north-western Singapore. The school combines the national curriculum with values-based character development and a distinctive sports and applied-learning culture.

Springfield offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school stands out for its rockwall climbing facility and programme, its taekwondo tradition, and a STEM and floorball culture that rounds out its co-curricular offerings.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Springfield offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Springfield is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Springfield official website](https://www.springfieldsec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning and Outdoor Sports

Springfield runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in STEM, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on outdoor sports and character development. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Springfield Secondary co-educational?

Yes. Springfield Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Springfield offer IP?

No. Springfield offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Springfield?

Springfield's COP differs by track and changes each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Springfield offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Springfield participates in DSA-Sec across floorball, rockwall climbing, taekwondo, and STEM. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Springfield?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Springfield if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
