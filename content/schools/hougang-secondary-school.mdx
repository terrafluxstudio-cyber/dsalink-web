Hougang Secondary School — commonly known as HGSS — is a government co-educational school located on Hougang Street 93 in the Hougang area of north-eastern Singapore. The school combines the national curriculum with values-based character development and an applied-learning emphasis in STEM.

Hougang offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its ensemble and debating culture, its judo and team-sports tradition, and an active community leadership focus.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Hougang offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Hougang is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Hougang official website](https://www.hougangsec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning and Leadership

Hougang runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in STEM, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on community youth leadership. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Campus and Facilities

Hougang Secondary School is located at **2 Hougang Street 93, Singapore 534256** in the Hougang area of north-eastern Singapore.

**Nearest MRT:**
- **Hougang** (North East Line NE14) — a short bus ride away
- **Buangkok** (North East Line NE15) — a short bus ride away, with Sengkang LRT connections nearby

Campus facilities include general and specialist classrooms, science laboratories, a multi-purpose hall, performing arts and music spaces, and sports fields and courts. For current facility details and a campus map, refer to the [Hougang official website](https://www.hougangsec.moe.edu.sg/).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Hougang Secondary co-educational?

Yes. Hougang Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Hougang offer IP?

No. Hougang offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Hougang?

Hougang is competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Hougang offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Hougang participates in DSA-Sec across sports, performing arts, and academic talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Hougang?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Hougang if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
