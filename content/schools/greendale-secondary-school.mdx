Greendale Secondary School is a government co-educational school located on Edgefield Plains in the Punggol area of north-eastern Singapore. The school combines the national curriculum with values-based character development and a strong emphasis on outdoor education, design and technology, and student leadership.

Greendale offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its outdoor adventure tradition, its Design, Technology and Engineering (DTE) focus, and a leadership development culture that anchors student growth beyond the academic curriculum.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Greendale offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Greendale is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Greendale official website](https://www.greendale.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning and Outdoor Education

Greendale runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in Design, Technology and Engineering, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on outdoor education and student leadership. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Greendale Secondary co-educational?

Yes. Greendale Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Greendale offer IP?

No. Greendale offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Greendale?

Greendale's COP differs by track and changes each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Greendale offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Greendale participates in DSA-Sec across leadership, Design Technology and Engineering, and outdoor adventure talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Greendale?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Greendale if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
