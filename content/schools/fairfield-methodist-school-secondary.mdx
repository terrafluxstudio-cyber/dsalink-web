Fairfield Methodist School (Secondary) — commonly known as Fairfield or FMS(S) — is a government-aided, autonomous, co-educational Methodist school located on Dover Road in the Queenstown area of western Singapore. As an autonomous school with a Methodist mission heritage, Fairfield combines the national curriculum with values-based character development.

Fairfield offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is part of the Fairfield Methodist family, which includes an affiliated primary school, and is known for its band, choir, and an active co-curricular culture grounded in its Methodist ethos.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Fairfield offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Fairfield is a mainstream autonomous school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Fairfield official website](https://www.fairfieldmethodistsec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Methodist Character and School Programmes

As a Methodist school, Fairfield runs values-based character education and student development programmes alongside its academic curriculum. These are school-based signature programmes rather than MOE-designated national electives. For the current list of the school's distinctive programmes, refer to the official website.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Admission Pathways

### PSLE and S1 Posting

Fairfield admits students through the S1 posting exercise across its Express and Normal posting bands. As an affiliated school within the Fairfield Methodist family, students from the affiliated primary school may receive affiliation priority in posting, subject to MOE's rules. The cut-off point (COP) differs by track and by affiliation status, and changes each year. For current and historical PSLE COP data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### DSA-Sec Admission

Fairfield participates in DSA-Sec across sports and performing arts talent areas. As a co-educational Methodist school with a strong band, choir, dance, and team sports tradition, Fairfield's DSA tends to attract applicants across these strengths. DSA offers place students into the school's mainstream tracks.

The specific talent areas offered change from year to year. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) for the current year's full list. For key application dates, see the [DSA-Sec Timeline](/timeline). For preparation by talent area, see [DSA Interview Preparation](/dsa-interview). ## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Fairfield co-educational?

Yes. Fairfield Methodist School (Secondary) is a co-educational school.

### Does Fairfield offer IP?

No. Fairfield offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### Does my child get priority from Fairfield Methodist primary school?

Fairfield secondary is affiliated to Fairfield Methodist primary, and affiliation priority may apply in the S1 posting exercise subject to MOE's rules. For DSA-Sec, admission is governed by MOE's centralised DSA framework and talent-based selection, separate from affiliation priority. Check the [Fairfield admissions page](https://www.fairfieldmethodistsec.moe.edu.sg/admissions/) for current details.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Fairfield?

Fairfield is an autonomous school competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and affiliation status and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Fairfield offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Fairfield participates in DSA-Sec across sports and performing arts talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Fairfield?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Fairfield if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices, with affiliation priority applying where relevant. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
