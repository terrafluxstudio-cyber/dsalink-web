Fuhua Secondary School — commonly known as FHSS — is a government co-educational school located on Jurong West Street 41 in the Jurong West area of western Singapore. The school combines the national curriculum with values-based character development and an applied-learning emphasis in STEM.

Fuhua offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is distinctive for its forensic-science applied-learning focus, alongside a Chinese orchestra and band culture and a shooting and team-sports tradition.

---

## What makes Fuhua distinctive

Fuhua has a genuinely distinctive applied-science identity: an Applied Learning Programme in forensic science — Forensic Science@Fuhua — a hook few schools can match for a science-curious child. Alongside that, the school carries a Chinese orchestra and concert band culture and a shooting and tchoukball tradition. For DSA-Sec families, the forensic-science strand and competitive shooting — a precision sport — are the clearest draws, together with music and team sports. The best fit is a child drawn to hands-on, investigative science, a precision-sport or niche-sport talent, or a musical strength. Sitting in Jurong West, Fuhua serves western families as a mainstream Express and Normal school, so a DSA offer places students into those tracks. Confirm this year's talent areas in the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder).

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Fuhua offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Fuhua is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Fuhua official website](https://www.fuhuasec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning (Forensic Science) and Leadership

Fuhua runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in STEM with an emerging-technologies focus, anchored by its Forensic Science@Fuhua applied-sciences programme, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on community youth leadership. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Fuhua Secondary co-educational?

Yes. Fuhua Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Fuhua offer IP?

No. Fuhua offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Fuhua?

Fuhua is competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Fuhua offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Fuhua participates in DSA-Sec across sports, performing arts, and academic or applied-science talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Fuhua?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Fuhua if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
