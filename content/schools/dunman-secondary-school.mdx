Dunman Secondary School — commonly known as DSS — is a government, autonomous, co-educational school located on Tampines Street 45 in the Tampines area of eastern Singapore. As an autonomous school, Dunman Secondary has greater flexibility over curriculum enrichment and co-curricular programming than standard government schools, within MOE's national framework. It is a separate school from Dunman High School.

Dunman Secondary offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its band and choral culture, its STEM focus, and an active team-sports tradition.

---

## What makes Dunman Secondary distinctive

Dunman Secondary is an autonomous school — a separate school from Dunman High — so it has wider curricular and co-curricular flexibility than standard government schools. Its identity blends a band-and-choral culture with a STEM focus. For DSA-Sec families, that draws musical talent in concert band and choir alongside competitive basketball, football, netball and volleyball. The best fit is a child with a musical or team-sport strength, or a STEM interest, who would value an autonomous school's added programme flexibility. Sitting in Tampines, Dunman Secondary serves eastern families as a mainstream Express and Normal school, so a DSA offer places students into those tracks. Because talent areas vary year to year, confirm the current list in the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder).

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Dunman Secondary offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Dunman Secondary is a mainstream autonomous school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Dunman Secondary official website](https://www.dunmansec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### School Programmes

As an autonomous school, Dunman Secondary runs curriculum enrichment and student development programmes, including a STEM and applied-learning emphasis, alongside the mainstream curriculum. These are school-based programmes; for the current list of the school's distinctive programmes, refer to the official website.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Dunman Secondary the same as Dunman High School?

No. Dunman Secondary School and Dunman High School are separate schools. Dunman Secondary is a mainstream autonomous school in Tampines offering the Express and Normal tracks; Dunman High is a SAP Integrated Programme school in the Tanjong Rhu area.

### Is Dunman Secondary co-educational?

Yes. Dunman Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Dunman Secondary offer IP?

No. Dunman Secondary offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Dunman Secondary?

Dunman Secondary is an autonomous school competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Dunman Secondary offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Dunman Secondary participates in DSA-Sec across performing arts, sports, and academic talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Dunman Secondary?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Dunman Secondary if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
