Edgefield Secondary School — commonly known as EFSS — is a government co-educational school located on Punggol Field in the Punggol area of north-eastern Singapore. The school combines the national curriculum with values-based character development and an applied-learning emphasis in business and entrepreneurship.

Edgefield offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its business and entrepreneurship focus, its taekwondo and team-sports tradition, and an active sports and outdoor education culture.

---

## What makes Edgefield distinctive

Edgefield has a clear enterprise-and-sport identity. Its Applied Learning Programme in business and entrepreneurship — Growing the Edgefield Mindset — develops enterprising, problem-solving thinking, while its Learning for Life Programme builds character through sport, anchored on taekwondo. For DSA-Sec families, the draws include competitive taekwondo and wushu — precision sports few schools pair — alongside basketball, softball and volleyball, and the school's entrepreneurship strand. The best fit is a child with a martial-arts or team-sport talent, or an enterprising, problem-solving streak, rather than a purely academic setting. Sitting in Punggol, Edgefield serves north-eastern families as a mainstream Express and Normal school, so a DSA offer places students into those tracks. ## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Edgefield offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Edgefield is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Edgefield official website](https://www.edgefieldsec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning and Outdoor Education

Edgefield runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in business and entrepreneurship, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on sports and outdoor education. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Edgefield Secondary co-educational?

Yes. Edgefield Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Edgefield offer IP?

No. Edgefield offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Edgefield?

Edgefield is competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Edgefield offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Edgefield participates in DSA-Sec across sports, leadership, and academic talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Edgefield?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Edgefield if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
