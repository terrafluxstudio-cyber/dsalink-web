Northland Secondary School — commonly known as NLSS — is a government co-educational school located on Yishun Street 22 in the Yishun area of northern Singapore. The school combines the national curriculum with values-based character development and an applied-learning emphasis in STEM.

Northland offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its hockey and wushu tradition, its team-sports culture, and an applied-learning and outdoor education focus.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Northland offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Northland is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Northland official website](https://www.northlandsec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning and Outdoor Education

Northland runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in STEM focused on health and food science, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on sports and outdoor education. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Northland Secondary co-educational?

Yes. Northland Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Northland offer IP?

No. Northland offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Northland?

Northland is competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Northland offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Northland participates in DSA-Sec across sports, academic, and performing arts talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Northland?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Northland if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
