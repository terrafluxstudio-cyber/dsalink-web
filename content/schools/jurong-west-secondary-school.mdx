Jurong West Secondary School — commonly known as JWSS — is a government co-educational school located on Jurong West Street 65 in the Jurong West area of western Singapore. The school combines the national curriculum with values-based character development and an applied-learning emphasis in the arts and technology.

Jurong West offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its visual arts and uniformed-group tradition, its STEM and applied-learning focus, and an active community leadership culture.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Jurong West offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Jurong West is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Jurong West official website](https://www.jurongwestsec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning and the Arts

Jurong West runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in aesthetics — harnessing technology in authentic contexts — and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on community youth leadership. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Jurong West Secondary co-educational?

Yes. Jurong West Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Jurong West offer IP?

No. Jurong West offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Jurong West?

Jurong West is competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Jurong West offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Jurong West participates in DSA-Sec across visual arts, sports, and academic or leadership talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Jurong West?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Jurong West if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
