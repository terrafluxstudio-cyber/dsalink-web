Bukit Panjang Govt. High School — commonly known as BPGHS — is a government autonomous co-educational school located on Choa Chu Kang Avenue 4 in the north-west of Singapore. As an autonomous school, BPGHS has greater flexibility over curriculum enrichment and co-curricular programming than standard government schools, within MOE's national framework.

BPGHS offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is distinctive for offering the Art Elective Programme (AEP), one of MOE's national art development pathways, alongside Malay language elective programmes — a combination that gives BPGHS a strong visual arts and language identity.

---

## What makes Bukit Panjang Govt. High distinctive

Bukit Panjang Govt. High has a dual visual-arts-and-language identity uncommon for a neighbourhood school. As an autonomous school it offers the Art Elective Programme (AEP) — an MOE-designated pathway available at only a small number of schools, where art is studied as a formal academic subject — alongside Malay language elective programmes. For DSA-Sec families, the AEP is a genuine draw for visual-arts talent and a recognised route towards art study at junior college. Beyond art, BPGHS carries a Chinese orchestra and concert band tradition and competitive floorball and netball, so its intake spans visual arts, music and sport. The strongest fit is a child with a serious artistic portfolio or clear direction, a language-and-culture interest, or a competitive team-sport strength. As an autonomous Express and Normal school in the north-west, a DSA offer places students into those tracks. Confirm this year's talent areas in the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder).

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

BPGHS offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: BPGHS is a mainstream autonomous school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [BPGHS official website](https://www.bpghs.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Art Elective Programme (AEP)

BPGHS offers the Art Elective Programme (AEP), an MOE-designated programme available at a small number of secondary schools. AEP students study art as a formal academic subject, developing studio practice and art history alongside the mainstream curriculum. AEP is a recognised DSA-Sec talent pathway and a route towards art study at the junior college level.

### Malay Language Programmes (EMAS and LEP)

BPGHS also offers the Elective Programme in Malay Language for Secondary Schools (EMAS) and the Language Elective Programme (Malay), MOE-designated programmes for students with strong Malay language ability.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is BPGHS co-educational?

Yes. Bukit Panjang Govt. High School is a co-educational school.

### Does BPGHS offer IP?

No. BPGHS offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What is the Art Elective Programme (AEP) at BPGHS?

The Art Elective Programme (AEP) is an MOE-designated programme where students study art as a formal academic subject, covering studio practice and art history. It is offered at a small number of secondary schools. AEP is a recognised DSA-Sec talent pathway — students with strong visual arts ability can apply through DSA-Sec for the art talent area. Check the [BPGHS DSA admissions page](https://www.bpghs.moe.edu.sg/admissions/) for the current year's criteria, and see [DSA Interview Preparation](/dsa-interview) for general guidance.

### What PSLE score does my child need for BPGHS?

BPGHS is an autonomous school competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does BPGHS offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. BPGHS participates in DSA-Sec across visual arts (AEP), performing arts, sports, and language talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at BPGHS?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to BPGHS if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
