St. Gabriel's Secondary School — commonly known as SGSS — is a government-aided Catholic boys' school located on Serangoon Avenue 1 in the Serangoon area of north-east Singapore. Run in the tradition of the Montfort Brothers of St Gabriel, the school is part of the St Gabriel's Foundation family and combines the national curriculum with values-based character development. St. Gabriel's is affiliated to St Gabriel's Primary School.

St. Gabriel's offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its strong sporting and uniformed-group traditions, its applied-learning focus in STEM, and an active community leadership culture.

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

St. Gabriel's offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: St. Gabriel's is a mainstream affiliated school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [St. Gabriel's official website](https://www.stgabrielssec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning and Leadership

St. Gabriel's runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in STEM focused on the future of transportation, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on community youth leadership. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Admission Pathways

### PSLE and S1 Posting

St. Gabriel's admits students through the S1 posting exercise across its Express and Normal posting bands. As an affiliated school, students from St Gabriel's Primary School may receive affiliation priority in posting, subject to MOE's rules. The cut-off point (COP) differs by track and by affiliation status, and changes each year. For current and historical PSLE COP data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### DSA-Sec Admission

St. Gabriel's participates in DSA-Sec across sports, performing arts, and uniformed-group or leadership talent areas. As a boys' school with a strong football, floorball, judo, and table-tennis tradition alongside a band and an active uniformed-group culture, St. Gabriel's DSA tends to attract applicants across these strengths. DSA offers place students into the school's mainstream tracks.

The specific talent areas offered change from year to year. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) for the current year's full list. For key application dates, see the [DSA-Sec Timeline](/timeline). For preparation by talent area, see [DSA Interview Preparation](/dsa-interview). For historical results context, see [DSA Results](/dsa-results).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is St. Gabriel's only for boys?

Yes. St. Gabriel's Secondary School is a single-sex school for boys.

### Does St. Gabriel's offer IP?

No. St. Gabriel's offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### Does my son get priority from St Gabriel's Primary School?

St. Gabriel's secondary is affiliated to St Gabriel's Primary School, and affiliation priority may apply in the S1 posting exercise subject to MOE's rules. For DSA-Sec, admission is governed by MOE's centralised DSA framework and talent-based selection, separate from affiliation priority. Check the [St. Gabriel's admissions page](https://www.stgabrielssec.moe.edu.sg/admissions/) for current details.

### What PSLE score does my son need for St. Gabriel's?

St. Gabriel's is competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and affiliation status and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does St. Gabriel's offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. St. Gabriel's participates in DSA-Sec across sports, performing arts, and leadership talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my son is not offered a DSA place at St. Gabriel's?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to St. Gabriel's if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices, with affiliation priority applying where relevant. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
