Spectra Secondary School is a government co-educational specialised school located in the Woodlands area of northern Singapore. The school offers an applied learning programme designed for students who learn best through practical, hands-on, and vocational approaches rather than the standard academic curriculum.

Spectra does not follow the mainstream Express or Normal academic tracks. Students pursue a customised applied learning curriculum with vocational modules that develop real-world skills and prepare them for further education and training at ITE or polytechnic pathways. Spectra does not currently offer DSA-Sec.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Applied Learning Programme

Spectra offers a specialised applied learning curriculum rather than the mainstream Express or Normal tracks. The programme integrates academic subjects with vocational and project-based modules, developing both practical skills and foundational knowledge in an applied context.

For P6 families: Spectra is a specialised school, not a mainstream secondary school. It is suited for students who learn best through experiential, applied, and vocational approaches. Refer to the [Spectra official website](https://www.spectra.edu.sg/) for the current curriculum structure and admission criteria.

### Vocational and Project-Based Modules

Spectra's curriculum includes specialist vocational modules across areas such as hospitality, design, business, and technology. These are integrated with the academic programme to provide a coherent, applied learning experience that develops students as confident and capable learners.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Admission Pathways

### School-Based Admission

Spectra Secondary School is a specialised school. Admission is not through the standard PSLE COP and S1 posting exercise. Students are referred or selected based on their learning profile and suitability for the applied learning approach. Refer to the [Spectra official website](https://www.spectra.edu.sg/) and [MOE school information](https://www.moe.gov.sg) for current admission criteria.

### DSA-Sec Admission

Spectra does not currently participate in DSA-Sec. Admission is through school-based selection. For the latest information on whether DSA-Sec is offered, use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What makes Spectra Secondary different from mainstream schools?

Spectra is a specialised school for students who learn best through applied, vocational, and project-based approaches. Rather than the standard Express or Normal curriculum, Spectra offers a customised applied learning programme with specialist vocational modules across areas such as hospitality, design, and technology.

### Is Spectra a government school?

Yes. Spectra Secondary School is a government co-educational school operated under MOE.

### How is admission to Spectra different from standard S1 posting?

Admission to Spectra is not through the standard PSLE COP and S1 posting exercise. Students are referred or selected based on their learning profile. Refer to the [Spectra official website](https://www.spectra.edu.sg/) for current admission information.

### Does Spectra offer DSA-Sec?

Spectra does not currently offer DSA-Sec. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to confirm the latest status.

### What are the options after Spectra Secondary?

Spectra's programme prepares students for further education and training, including ITE and vocational pathways. Refer to the [Spectra official website](https://www.spectra.edu.sg/) for post-secondary progression information.
