Bartley Secondary School — commonly known as BTSS — is a government co-educational school located on Jalan Bunga Rampai in the Bartley area of central-east Singapore. It offers the national curriculum with a focus on applied learning in the sciences.

Bartley offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is distinctive for its applied-learning focus in chemical and applied sciences — including a fragrance and health and food science programme — alongside an active sports and leadership culture.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express, Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical)

Bartley offers all three mainstream secondary courses. Express students prepare for the GCE O-Level examinations over four years; Normal (Academic) and Normal (Technical) students follow the N-Level pathways with progression options. The school implements full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Bartley is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Bartley official website](https://www.bartleysec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning (Chemical and Applied Sciences)

Bartley runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in STEM with a focus on health and food science and applied chemistry — including a distinctive fragrance and chemical sciences strand — and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on community youth leadership. These MOE programmes anchor the school's applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Bartley co-educational?

Yes. Bartley Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Bartley offer IP?

No. Bartley offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What is the applied sciences programme at Bartley?

Bartley runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in STEM with a focus on health, food science, and applied chemistry — including a distinctive fragrance and chemical sciences strand. An applied sciences talent pathway may also be available through DSA-Sec; check the [Bartley DSA admissions page](https://www.bartleysec.moe.edu.sg/admissions/) and the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) for the current year's confirmed talent areas.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Bartley?

Bartley is competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Bartley offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Bartley participates in DSA-Sec across sports, academic or applied sciences, and leadership talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Bartley?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Bartley if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
