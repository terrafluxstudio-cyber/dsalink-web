Tanjong Katong Girls' School — commonly known as TKGS — is a government autonomous school for girls located on Dunman Lane in the Marine Parade / Paya Lebar area of eastern Singapore. As an autonomous school, TKGS has greater flexibility over curriculum enrichment and co-curricular programming than standard government schools, within MOE's national framework.

TKGS offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its Music Elective Programme (MEP), its strong performing arts and sports culture, and an established tradition of all-round development for girls.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

TKGS offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: TKGS is a mainstream autonomous school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [TKGS official website](https://www.tkgs.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Music Elective Programme (MEP)

TKGS offers the Music Elective Programme (MEP), an MOE-designated programme available at a small number of secondary schools. MEP students study music as a formal academic subject, with DSA-Sec applications available for the MEP talent area. MEP at the secondary level is a pathway towards the Higher Music Programme at the junior college level.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

### PSLE and S1 Posting

TKGS admits students through the S1 posting exercise across its Express and Normal posting bands. The cut-off point (COP) differs by track and changes each year. For current and historical PSLE COP data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is TKGS only for girls?

Yes. Tanjong Katong Girls' School is a single-sex school for girls.

### Does TKGS offer IP?

No. TKGS offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my daughter need for TKGS?

TKGS is an autonomous school competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does TKGS offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. TKGS participates in DSA-Sec across sports, performing arts, and music (MEP) talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What is the MEP at TKGS and how does DSA for MEP work?

The Music Elective Programme (MEP) is an MOE-designated programme where students study music as a formal academic subject. DSA-Sec applications for the MEP talent area at TKGS typically involve an audition and/or interview. Read the [TKGS DSA admissions page](https://www.tkgs.moe.edu.sg/admissions/) for the specific year's criteria, and see [DSA Interview Preparation](/dsa-interview) for general guidance.

### What happens if my daughter is not offered a DSA place at TKGS?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to TKGS if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
