CHIJ St. Theresa's Convent — commonly known as STC — is a government-aided, Catholic school for girls located on Lower Delta Road in the Bukit Merah area of southern Singapore. Part of the CHIJ (Convent of the Holy Infant Jesus) family of schools, it combines the national curriculum with values-based character development in the CHIJ tradition.

STC offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its strong hockey and team-sports tradition, its dance and drama culture, and an active community service ethos.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

STC offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: STC is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [STC official website](https://www.chijsttheresasconvent.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning and Character Development

STC runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) focused on portable skills through real-world problem solving, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on sports and outdoor education, alongside the values-based character education of the CHIJ tradition. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

STC admits students through the S1 posting exercise across its Express and Normal posting bands. The cut-off point (COP) differs by track and changes each year. For current and historical PSLE COP data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is CHIJ St. Theresa's Convent only for girls?

Yes. CHIJ St. Theresa's Convent is a single-sex school for girls.

### Does STC offer IP?

No. STC offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my daughter need for STC?

STC is competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does STC offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. STC participates in DSA-Sec across sports and performing arts talent areas, with a particularly strong hockey tradition. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my daughter is not offered a DSA place at STC?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to STC if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
