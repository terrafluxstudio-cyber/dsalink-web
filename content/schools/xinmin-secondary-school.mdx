Xinmin Secondary School — commonly known as XSS — is a government autonomous co-educational school located on Hougang Avenue 8 in the north-east of Singapore. As an autonomous school, Xinmin has greater flexibility over curriculum enrichment and co-curricular programming than standard government schools, within MOE's national framework.

Xinmin offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its strong applied learning and STEM culture — anchored by its school-based MAKERS@Xinmin programme — alongside a broad performing arts and sports offering and a wide range of uniformed groups.

---

## What makes Xinmin distinctive

Xinmin is an autonomous school, so it has wider curricular and co-curricular flexibility than standard government schools. Its identity is STEM-and-making, anchored by its school-based MAKERS@Xinmin programme, alongside a broad performing-arts and sports offering. For DSA-Sec families, that draws a STEM-and-design strength together with competitive shooting and rock-wall climbing — niche sports few schools support — floorball and volleyball, and a Chinese dance, guzheng, choir and concert band culture. The best fit is a child with a hands-on STEM or making strength, a precision or adventure-sport talent, or a performing-arts strength. Sitting in Hougang, Xinmin serves north-eastern families as a mainstream Express and Normal school, so a DSA offer places students into those tracks. Confirm this year's talent areas in the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder).

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Xinmin offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Xinmin is a mainstream autonomous school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Xinmin official website](https://www.xinminsec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### MAKERS@Xinmin (school-based STEM)

Xinmin runs MAKERS@Xinmin, a school-based applied-learning and STEM programme that gives students hands-on experience with design, making, and emerging technologies. This is a school signature programme rather than an MOE-designated national elective. For current programme details, refer to the official website.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Xinmin co-educational?

Yes. Xinmin Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Xinmin offer IP?

No. Xinmin offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Xinmin?

Xinmin is an autonomous school competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### What is MAKERS@Xinmin?

MAKERS@Xinmin is the school's signature applied-learning and STEM programme, giving students hands-on experience with design, making, and emerging technologies. It is a school-based programme rather than an MOE-designated national elective. A STEM-related talent pathway may be available through DSA-Sec — check the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) for the current year's confirmed talent areas.

### Does Xinmin offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Xinmin participates in DSA-Sec across sports, performing arts, and uniformed group talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Xinmin?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Xinmin if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
