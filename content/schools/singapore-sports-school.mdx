Singapore Sports School — commonly known as SSP — is one of Singapore's specialised independent schools, dedicated to developing student-athletes who pursue elite sport alongside their academic education. It is a co-educational, largely residential (boarding) school that integrates a customised academic programme with high-performance sports training and competition.

Singapore Sports School is distinctive in how students are admitted: **it does not participate in either the DSA-Sec exercise or the Secondary 1 (S1) Posting Exercise.** As stated on MOE SchoolFinder, students are admitted through the school's own school-based admission process — sport-specific selection and trials run directly by the school. This makes its pathway different from every mainstream secondary school and even from the other specialised schools.

The school is located on Champions Way in the Woodlands area of northern Singapore, on a purpose-built campus with sporting, academic, and boarding facilities designed around the demands of a student-athlete's schedule.

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Academics Integrated with Elite Sport

Singapore Sports School offers a through-train programme that combines academic study with structured, high-performance sports training. Students sit national examinations (such as the GCE O-Level) within a customised academic structure designed to accommodate training loads, competition calendars, and overseas tournaments.

For P6 families: SSP is an environment for children who are already committed, talented athletes and want to pursue sport seriously without giving up their academic progression. Refer to the [Singapore Sports School official website](https://www.sportsschool.edu.sg/) for the current academic pathways and qualifications offered.

### Sports Programmes

SSP's core is its sports academies, which develop athletes across a range of sports through professional coaching, sports science, and competition exposure. The specific sports offered and their entry standards are set by the school and updated each cycle. The [official website](https://www.sportsschool.edu.sg/) lists the current sports and their selection requirements.

For context on the general DSA-Sec process at other schools, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Admission Pathways

### School-Based Sports Selection (not DSA-Sec, not S1 Posting)

Singapore Sports School admits students through its own school-based admission process, centred on sport-specific selection and trials. It does not take part in DSA-Sec or the S1 Posting Exercise. Families apply directly to the school, and selection is based on athletic ability and potential in the chosen sport, together with the school's academic and developmental criteria.

Because SSP runs its own selection, the application timing and requirements are set by the school rather than the national DSA-Sec calendar. Check the [Singapore Sports School admissions page](https://www.sportsschool.edu.sg/admissions) for the current cycle's sports, trial dates, and criteria.

### How SSP differs from a DSA-Sec sports route

At mainstream secondary schools, a talented athlete typically applies through DSA-Sec for a sports talent area and is placed into the school's mainstream tracks. SSP is different: it is a full sports-specialised school with its own admission, academic structure, and boarding. Families weighing a DSA-Sec sports pathway at a mainstream school against SSP should consider the level of athletic commitment and the residential, training-centred environment SSP involves. For the DSA-Sec route at other schools, use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Singapore Sports School co-educational?

Yes. Singapore Sports School is a co-educational school, and it is largely residential (students board on campus).

### Does my child apply to SSP through DSA-Sec?

No. Unlike mainstream schools, SSP does not admit through DSA-Sec or the S1 Posting Exercise. Families apply directly to the school, and selection is through its own sport-specific trials and admission process. Check the [SSP admissions page](https://www.sportsschool.edu.sg/admissions) for the current cycle.

### What academic qualifications does SSP offer?

SSP integrates academics with elite sport and prepares students for national examinations such as the GCE O-Level within a customised through-train structure. For the exact current pathways and qualifications, refer to the [official website](https://www.sportsschool.edu.sg/).

### What PSLE score does my child need for SSP?

SSP selects primarily on athletic ability and potential through its own trials rather than a PSLE cut-off. Academic and developmental criteria also apply. See the [official admissions page](https://www.sportsschool.edu.sg/admissions) for current requirements.

### Is SSP the only way for a talented young athlete to continue in sport?

No. Many mainstream secondary schools offer sports talent areas through DSA-Sec, where a student trains and competes while in the school's mainstream academic tracks. SSP is the fully sports-specialised, residential option. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see sports talent areas at other schools.
