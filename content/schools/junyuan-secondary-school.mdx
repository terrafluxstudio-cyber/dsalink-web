Junyuan Secondary School is a government co-educational school located on Tampines Street 71 in the Tampines area of eastern Singapore. The school combines the national curriculum with values-based character development and a strong team-sports and community leadership tradition.

Junyuan offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its rugby and basketball culture, its volleyball tradition, and a community youth leadership focus that develops students as active contributors beyond the classroom.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Junyuan offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Junyuan is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Junyuan official website](https://www.junyuansec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning and Community Leadership

Junyuan runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in a STEM or applied-learning focus, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on community youth leadership and sports. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Junyuan Secondary co-educational?

Yes. Junyuan Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Junyuan offer IP?

No. Junyuan offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Junyuan?

Junyuan's COP differs by track and changes each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Junyuan offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Junyuan participates in DSA-Sec across community youth leadership, basketball, rugby, and volleyball. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Junyuan?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Junyuan if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
