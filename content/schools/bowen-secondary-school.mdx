Bowen Secondary School — commonly known as BWSS — is a government co-educational school located on Lorong Napiri in the Hougang / Serangoon North area of north-east Singapore. It offers the national curriculum with a focus on applied learning and student development.

Bowen offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is distinctive for its applied-learning focus in business and entrepreneurship, alongside a strong marching band and an active sports tradition.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express, Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical)

Bowen offers all three mainstream secondary courses. Express students prepare for the GCE O-Level examinations over four years; Normal (Academic) and Normal (Technical) students follow the N-Level pathways with progression options. The school implements full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Bowen is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Bowen official website](https://www.bowensec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning (Business and Entrepreneurship)

Bowen runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in business and entrepreneurship, giving students hands-on experience with enterprise, problem-solving, and innovation, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on community youth leadership. These MOE programmes anchor the school's applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Bowen co-educational?

Yes. Bowen Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Bowen offer IP?

No. Bowen offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What is the entrepreneurship programme at Bowen?

Bowen runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in business and entrepreneurship, giving students hands-on experience with enterprise and innovation. An entrepreneurship talent pathway may also be available through DSA-Sec; check the [Bowen DSA admissions page](https://www.bowensec.moe.edu.sg/admissions/) and the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) for the current year's confirmed talent areas.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Bowen?

Bowen is competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Bowen offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Bowen participates in DSA-Sec across sports, performing arts, academic or entrepreneurship, and leadership talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Bowen?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Bowen if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
