Greenridge Secondary School is a government co-educational school located on Choa Chu Kang Drive in the Choa Chu Kang area of north-western Singapore. The school combines the national curriculum with values-based character development and a broad programme spanning humanities, sports, and student leadership.

Greenridge offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its rugby tradition, its debating and public speaking culture, and an active media and journalism programme that develops student voice and communication skills.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Greenridge offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Greenridge is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Greenridge official website](https://www.greenridgesec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning and Student Voice

Greenridge runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) with a focus on communications and humanities, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on community youth leadership and student development. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Greenridge Secondary co-educational?

Yes. Greenridge Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Greenridge offer IP?

No. Greenridge offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Greenridge?

Greenridge's COP differs by track and changes each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Greenridge offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Greenridge participates in DSA-Sec across debating and public speaking, media and journalism, community youth leadership, and rugby. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Greenridge?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Greenridge if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
