CHIJ St. Joseph's Convent — commonly known as CHIJ SJC — is a government-aided, Catholic school for girls located on Sengkang East Way in the Sengkang area of north-eastern Singapore. Part of the CHIJ (Convent of the Holy Infant Jesus) family of schools, it combines the national curriculum with values-based character development in the CHIJ tradition.

CHIJ SJC offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its debate and theatre culture, its choral and band tradition, and an active team-sports following.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

CHIJ SJC offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: CHIJ SJC is a mainstream affiliated school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [CHIJ SJC official website](https://www.chijstjosephsconvent.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning and Character Development

CHIJ SJC runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in the art of communication through theatre and debate, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on community service and leadership, alongside the values-based character education of the CHIJ tradition. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Admission Pathways

### PSLE and S1 Posting

CHIJ SJC admits students through the S1 posting exercise across its Express and Normal posting bands. As a CHIJ school, students from affiliated CHIJ primary schools may receive affiliation priority in posting, subject to MOE's rules. The cut-off point (COP) differs by track and by affiliation status, and changes each year. For current and historical PSLE COP data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### DSA-Sec Admission

CHIJ SJC participates in DSA-Sec across performing arts, sports, and academic talent areas. As a girls' school with a debate and theatre culture, a choral and band tradition, and a netball and team-sports following, CHIJ SJC's DSA tends to attract applicants across these strengths. DSA offers place students into the school's mainstream tracks.

The specific talent areas offered change from year to year. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) for the current year's full list. ## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is CHIJ St. Joseph's Convent only for girls?

Yes. CHIJ St. Joseph's Convent is a single-sex school for girls.

### Does CHIJ SJC offer IP?

No. CHIJ SJC offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### Does my daughter get affiliation priority at CHIJ SJC?

CHIJ SJC is part of the CHIJ family, and students from affiliated CHIJ primary schools may receive affiliation priority in the S1 posting exercise, subject to MOE's rules. For DSA-Sec, admission is governed by MOE's centralised DSA framework and talent-based selection, separate from affiliation priority. Check the [CHIJ SJC admissions page](https://www.chijstjosephsconvent.moe.edu.sg/admissions/) for current details.

### What PSLE score does my daughter need for CHIJ SJC?

CHIJ SJC is competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and affiliation status and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does CHIJ SJC offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. CHIJ SJC participates in DSA-Sec across performing arts, sports, and academic talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my daughter is not offered a DSA place at CHIJ SJC?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to CHIJ SJC if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices, with affiliation priority applying where relevant. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
