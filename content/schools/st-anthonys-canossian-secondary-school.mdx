St. Anthony's Canossian Secondary School — commonly known as SACSS — is a government-aided, autonomous, Catholic school for girls located on Bedok North Avenue 4 in eastern Singapore. Run in the Canossian tradition, the school combines the national curriculum with values-based character development. St. Anthony's Canossian Secondary is affiliated to St. Anthony's Canossian Primary School.

St. Anthony's Canossian offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its strong choral and ensemble tradition, its multicultural dance culture, and an active sports and community service ethos.

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

St. Anthony's Canossian offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: St. Anthony's Canossian is a mainstream affiliated school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [St. Anthony's Canossian official website](https://www.stanthonyscanossiansec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Canossian Character and School Programmes

In the Canossian tradition, St. Anthony's Canossian runs values-based character education and student development programmes alongside its academic curriculum. These are school-based signature programmes rather than MOE-designated national electives. For the current list of the school's distinctive programmes, refer to the official website.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Admission Pathways

### PSLE and S1 Posting

St. Anthony's Canossian admits students through the S1 posting exercise across its Express and Normal posting bands. As an affiliated school, students from St. Anthony's Canossian Primary School may receive affiliation priority in posting, subject to MOE's rules. The cut-off point (COP) differs by track and by affiliation status, and changes each year. For current and historical PSLE COP data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### DSA-Sec Admission

St. Anthony's Canossian participates in DSA-Sec across performing arts, sports, and academic talent areas. As a girls' school with a broad choral, band, and ensemble tradition, a multicultural dance culture, and a netball, floorball, and taekwondo following, St. Anthony's Canossian's DSA tends to attract applicants across these strengths. DSA offers place students into the school's mainstream tracks.

The specific talent areas offered change from year to year. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) for the current year's full list. For key application dates, see the [DSA-Sec Timeline](/timeline). For preparation by talent area, see [DSA Interview Preparation](/dsa-interview). ## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is St. Anthony's Canossian only for girls?

Yes. St. Anthony's Canossian Secondary School is a single-sex school for girls.

### Does St. Anthony's Canossian offer IP?

No. St. Anthony's Canossian offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### Does my daughter get priority from St. Anthony's Canossian Primary School?

St. Anthony's Canossian secondary is affiliated to St. Anthony's Canossian Primary School, and affiliation priority may apply in the S1 posting exercise subject to MOE's rules. For DSA-Sec, admission is governed by MOE's centralised DSA framework and talent-based selection, separate from affiliation priority. Check the [St. Anthony's Canossian admissions page](https://www.stanthonyscanossiansec.moe.edu.sg/admissions/) for current details.

### What PSLE score does my daughter need for St. Anthony's Canossian?

St. Anthony's Canossian is competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and affiliation status and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does St. Anthony's Canossian offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. St. Anthony's Canossian participates in DSA-Sec across performing arts, sports, and academic talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my daughter is not offered a DSA place at St. Anthony's Canossian?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to St. Anthony's Canossian if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices, with affiliation priority applying where relevant. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
