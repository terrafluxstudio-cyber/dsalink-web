Jurongville Secondary School — commonly known as JVSS — is a government co-educational school located on Jurong East Avenue 1 in the Jurong East area of western Singapore. The school combines the national curriculum with values-based character development and an applied-learning emphasis in STEM.

Jurongville offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its strong uniformed-group tradition, its tchoukball and team-sports culture, and a STEM and outdoor-education focus.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Jurongville offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Jurongville is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Jurongville official website](https://www.jurongvillesec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning and Outdoor Education

Jurongville runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in STEM with an emerging-technologies focus, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on sports and outdoor education. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Jurongville Secondary co-educational?

Yes. Jurongville Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Jurongville offer IP?

No. Jurongville offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Jurongville?

Jurongville is competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Jurongville offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Jurongville participates in DSA-Sec across uniformed-group, sports, and academic talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Jurongville?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Jurongville if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
