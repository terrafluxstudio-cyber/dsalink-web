Loyang View Secondary School — commonly known as LVSS — is a government co-educational school located on Pasir Ris Street 11 in the Pasir Ris area of eastern Singapore. The school combines the national curriculum with values-based character development and an applied-learning emphasis in STEM.

Loyang View offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its rock-climbing and shooting tradition, its STEM and design focus, and an active team-sports and outdoor culture.

---

## What makes Loyang View distinctive

Loyang View has a distinctive adventure-and-STEM identity. The school is known for a rock-climbing and shooting tradition — niche precision-and-adventure sports few schools support — while its Applied Learning Programme sits in STEM with a sustainability and design focus. For DSA-Sec families, those sports are the clearest draw, alongside competitive basketball, floorball and netball, and a design-and-engineering strand. The best fit is a child with a precision or adventure-sport strength — especially rock-climbing or shooting — a team-sport talent, or a hands-on STEM-and-design interest, rather than a purely academic setting. Sitting in Pasir Ris, Loyang View serves eastern families as a mainstream Express and Normal school, so a DSA offer places students into those tracks. ## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Loyang View offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Loyang View is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Loyang View official website](https://www.loyangviewsec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning and Outdoor Education

Loyang View runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in STEM with a design and engineering focus, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on student leadership through sports and outdoor education. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Loyang View Secondary co-educational?

Yes. Loyang View Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Loyang View offer IP?

No. Loyang View offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Loyang View?

Loyang View is competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Loyang View offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Loyang View participates in DSA-Sec across sports, academic or applied-technology, and performing arts talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Loyang View?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Loyang View if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
