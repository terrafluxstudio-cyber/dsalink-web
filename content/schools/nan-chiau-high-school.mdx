Nan Chiau High School — commonly known as NCHS — is a government-aided, SAP (Special Assistance Plan) co-educational school located in Anchorvale, Sengkang, in the north-east of Singapore. Founded in 1947 with roots in the Hokkien community, it is one of Singapore's SAP schools, offering Chinese as the first language alongside English within a bilingual, bicultural framework — all students read Higher Chinese from Secondary 1.

Nan Chiau offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) academic track with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its strong Chinese language and arts programmes, its applied learning in emerging technologies, and an active co-curricular culture.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express Track

Nan Chiau offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) academic track with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths. As a SAP school, Chinese is offered at a high level, with all students reading Higher Chinese from Secondary 1.

For P6 families: Nan Chiau is a mainstream SAP school rather than an IP school. Families seeking a SAP school with an IP pathway may consider Dunman High or River Valley High. Refer to the [Nan Chiau official website](https://www.nanchiauhigh.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Special Assistance Plan (SAP) and Applied Programmes

As a SAP school, Nan Chiau combines bilingual and bicultural development with the mainstream curriculum. The school offers the Language Elective Programme (Chinese) and an Enhanced Art Programme, an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in STEM with an emerging technologies focus, and a Learning for Life Programme centred on the arts.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Campus and Facilities

### PSLE and S1 Posting

Nan Chiau admits students through the S1 posting exercise under the SAP category, in its Express (PG3) posting band. The cut-off point (COP) differs by track and changes each year. For current and historical PSLE COP data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Nan Chiau co-educational?

Yes. Nan Chiau High School is a co-educational school.

### Is Nan Chiau a SAP school?

Yes. Nan Chiau is a SAP (Special Assistance Plan) school offering Chinese as the first language alongside English. All students read Higher Chinese from Secondary 1. Students are expected to have good Chinese language proficiency.

### Does Nan Chiau offer IP?

No. Nan Chiau offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) academic track. It is not an Integrated Programme school. Families seeking a SAP school with an IP pathway may consider Dunman High or River Valley High.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Nan Chiau?

Nan Chiau is a SAP school competitive for its Express posting band, with the COP changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Nan Chiau offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Nan Chiau participates in DSA-Sec across sports, performing arts, visual arts, and bilingualism talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Nan Chiau?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Nan Chiau if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
