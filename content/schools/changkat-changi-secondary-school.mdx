Changkat Changi Secondary School — commonly known as CCSS — is a government co-educational school located on Simei Street 3 in the Simei area of eastern Singapore. The school combines the national curriculum with values-based character development and an applied-learning emphasis in STEM.

Changkat Changi offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its aerospace and aviation focus, its band and Chinese instrumental culture, and an active track-and-field and team-sports tradition.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Changkat Changi offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Changkat Changi is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Changkat Changi official website](https://www.changkatchangisec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning and Leadership

Changkat Changi runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in STEM focused on the future of transportation, with an aerospace and aviation emphasis, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on community youth leadership. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Changkat Changi Secondary co-educational?

Yes. Changkat Changi Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Changkat Changi offer IP?

No. Changkat Changi offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Changkat Changi?

Changkat Changi is competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Changkat Changi offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Changkat Changi participates in DSA-Sec across sports, academic or applied-technology, and performing arts talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Changkat Changi?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Changkat Changi if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
