Yuying Secondary School is a government co-educational school located in the Hougang area of north-eastern Singapore. The school combines the national curriculum with values-based character development and a strong performing arts and team-sports tradition.

Yuying offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its concert band and wushu tradition, its volleyball culture, and a community youth leadership focus that develops students as engaged contributors beyond the academic curriculum.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Yuying offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Yuying is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Yuying official website](https://www.yuyingsec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning and Community Leadership

Yuying runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in a STEM or applied-learning focus, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on community youth leadership and performing arts. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Yuying Secondary co-educational?

Yes. Yuying Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Yuying offer IP?

No. Yuying offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Yuying?

Yuying's COP differs by track and changes each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Yuying offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Yuying participates in DSA-Sec across concert band, volleyball, wushu, and community youth leadership. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Yuying?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Yuying if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
