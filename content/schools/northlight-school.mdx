Northlight School is a government co-educational specialised school that provides an alternative education pathway for students who need more time to develop their learning foundation before progressing through the secondary education system. The school offers a supportive, student-centred environment built around individual progress and holistic development.

Northlight does not follow the mainstream secondary school tracks or the standard PSLE admission process. Students are referred to Northlight through MOE and follow the school's own certification programme. Northlight does not currently offer DSA-Sec.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Alternative Education Pathway

Northlight School offers a school-based certification programme rather than the standard O-Level or N-Level qualifications. The programme focuses on building a strong foundation in literacy, numeracy, and life skills, with an emphasis on character development and preparing students for further education and employment.

For P6 families: Northlight is an alternative pathway school for students who need a more tailored, supportive educational environment. Admission is through MOE referral rather than the standard PSLE COP and S1 posting process. Refer to the [Northlight official website](https://www.northlight.edu.sg/) for the current programme structure and entry criteria.

### Holistic Development and Life Skills

Northlight places a strong emphasis on holistic student development, including character education, social skills, and practical life skills alongside academic learning. The school's environment is designed to build student confidence and provide a positive learning experience.

For context on the secondary school system, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is Northlight School?

Northlight School is an alternative pathway school for students who need more time and support to develop their learning foundation. It provides a nurturing environment with a school-based certification programme focused on literacy, numeracy, life skills, and character development.

### Is Northlight a government school?

Yes. Northlight School is a government co-educational school under MOE.

### How is admission to Northlight different from standard S1 posting?

Admission to Northlight is through MOE referral rather than PSLE COP and standard S1 posting. Refer to the [Northlight official website](https://www.northlight.edu.sg/) for current entry criteria.

### Does Northlight offer DSA-Sec?

Northlight does not currently offer DSA-Sec. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to confirm the latest status.

### What are the options after Northlight School?

Northlight's programme prepares students for further education and employment pathways. Students may progress to ITE or other post-secondary options. Refer to the [Northlight official website](https://www.northlight.edu.sg/) for progression information.
