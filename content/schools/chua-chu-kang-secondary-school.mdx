Chua Chu Kang Secondary School is a government co-educational school located on Teck Whye Avenue in the Chua Chu Kang area of north-western Singapore. The school combines the national curriculum with values-based character development and an applied-learning emphasis in science and sustainability.

Chua Chu Kang offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its hockey tradition, its modern dance culture, and a community youth leadership focus that anchors student development beyond the academic curriculum.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Chua Chu Kang offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Chua Chu Kang is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Chua Chu Kang official website](https://www.chuachukangsec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning and Community Leadership

Chua Chu Kang runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in science for sustainable development, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on community youth leadership. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Chua Chu Kang Secondary co-educational?

Yes. Chua Chu Kang Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Chua Chu Kang offer IP?

No. Chua Chu Kang offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Chua Chu Kang?

Chua Chu Kang's COP differs by track and changes each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Chua Chu Kang offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Chua Chu Kang participates in DSA-Sec across leadership, performing arts, STEM, and sports talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Chua Chu Kang?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Chua Chu Kang if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
