CHIJ Katong Convent — commonly known as KC — is a government-aided, autonomous, Catholic school for girls located on Marine Terrace in the Marine Parade area of eastern Singapore. Part of the CHIJ (Convent of the Holy Infant Jesus) family of schools, it combines the national curriculum with values-based character development in the CHIJ tradition. As an autonomous school, KC has greater flexibility over curriculum enrichment and co-curricular programming than standard government schools.

KC offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is affiliated to CHIJ (Katong) Primary and is known for its broad performing arts culture, strong debating and drama, and an active sports tradition.

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

KC offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: KC is a mainstream autonomous school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [KC official website](https://chijkatongconvent.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Character and Arts Development

As a CHIJ school, KC runs values-based character education and a broad arts and student development programme alongside its academic curriculum. Its visual arts and music offerings are co-curricular and school-based rather than MOE-designated national electives. For the current list of the school's distinctive programmes, refer to the official website.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Admission Pathways

### PSLE and S1 Posting

KC admits students through the S1 posting exercise across its Express and Normal posting bands. As an affiliated school within the CHIJ family, students from CHIJ (Katong) Primary may receive affiliation priority in posting, subject to MOE's rules. The cut-off point (COP) differs by track and by affiliation status, and changes each year. For current and historical PSLE COP data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### DSA-Sec Admission

KC participates in DSA-Sec across performing arts, sports, and academic talent areas. As a girls' school with a broad band, orchestra, drama, and dance culture, a strong debating tradition, and competitive netball, floorball, and bowling, KC's DSA tends to attract applicants across these strengths. DSA offers place students into the school's mainstream tracks.

The specific talent areas offered change from year to year. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) for the current year's full list. For key application dates, see the [DSA-Sec Timeline](/timeline). For preparation by talent area, see [DSA Interview Preparation](/dsa-interview). ## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is CHIJ Katong Convent only for girls?

Yes. CHIJ Katong Convent is a single-sex school for girls.

### Does KC offer IP?

No. KC offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### Does my daughter get priority from CHIJ (Katong) Primary?

KC is affiliated to CHIJ (Katong) Primary, and affiliation priority may apply in the S1 posting exercise subject to MOE's rules. For DSA-Sec, admission is governed by MOE's centralised DSA framework and talent-based selection, separate from affiliation priority. Check the [KC admissions page](https://chijkatongconvent.moe.edu.sg/admissions/) for current details.

### What PSLE score does my daughter need for KC?

KC is an autonomous school competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and affiliation status and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does KC offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. KC participates in DSA-Sec across performing arts, sports, and academic talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my daughter is not offered a DSA place at KC?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to KC if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices, with affiliation priority applying where relevant. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
