Woodlands Ring Secondary School — commonly known as WRSS — is a government co-educational school in the Woodlands area of northern Singapore. The school combines the national curriculum with values-based character development and an applied-learning emphasis in STEM.

Woodlands Ring offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its hockey tradition, its STEAM and robotics focus, and an active band and dance culture.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Woodlands Ring offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Woodlands Ring is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Woodlands Ring official website](https://www.woodlandsringsec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning and STEAM

Woodlands Ring runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in STEM focused on the future of transportation, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on community youth leadership. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and STEAM identity beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Woodlands Ring Secondary co-educational?

Yes. Woodlands Ring Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Woodlands Ring offer IP?

No. Woodlands Ring offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Woodlands Ring?

Woodlands Ring is competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Woodlands Ring offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Woodlands Ring participates in DSA-Sec across sports, performing arts, and academic or applied-technology talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Woodlands Ring?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Woodlands Ring if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
