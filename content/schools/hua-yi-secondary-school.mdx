Hua Yi Secondary School — commonly known as HYSS — is a government co-educational school located on Jurong West Street 42 in the Jurong West area of western Singapore. The school combines the national curriculum with values-based character development and an applied-learning emphasis.

Hua Yi offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its strong instrumental and dance ensembles, its applied-learning focus in STEM, and an active sports and community leadership culture.

---

## What makes Hua Yi distinctive

Hua Yi has a clear performing-arts identity, built on strong instrumental and dance ensembles. The school carries a Chinese dance, guzheng, concert band and choir culture, while its Applied Learning Programme sits in STEM with a health and food-science focus. For DSA-Sec families, those performing-arts ensembles are genuine draws, alongside competitive basketball, football, netball, volleyball and track and field. The best fit is a child with a musical or dance talent — especially in a Chinese cultural-arts ensemble — or a team-sport strength, who would value a performing-arts-rich school. Sitting in Jurong West, Hua Yi serves western families as a mainstream Express and Normal school, so a DSA offer places students into those tracks. Confirm this year's talent areas in the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder).

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Hua Yi offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Hua Yi is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Hua Yi official website](https://www.huayisec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning and Leadership

Hua Yi runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in STEM focused on health and food science, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on community youth leadership. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Hua Yi co-educational?

Yes. Hua Yi Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Hua Yi offer IP?

No. Hua Yi offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Hua Yi?

Hua Yi is competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Hua Yi offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Hua Yi participates in DSA-Sec across sports, performing arts, and leadership talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Hua Yi?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Hua Yi if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
