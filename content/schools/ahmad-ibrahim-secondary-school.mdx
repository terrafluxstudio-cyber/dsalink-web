Ahmad Ibrahim Secondary School — commonly known as AISS — is a government co-educational school located on Yishun Avenue 7 in the Yishun area of northern Singapore. The school combines the national curriculum with values-based character development and an applied-learning emphasis in the humanities.

Ahmad Ibrahim offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its shooting and team-sports tradition, its music and media culture, and a media-literacy and outdoor-education focus.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Ahmad Ibrahim offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Ahmad Ibrahim is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Ahmad Ibrahim official website](https://www.ahmadibrahimsec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning and Outdoor Education

Ahmad Ibrahim runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in the humanities, focused on critical social inquiry and media literacy, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on character development through sports and outdoor learning. The school also offers a school-based Enhanced Music Programme as an elective; this is distinct from the MOE-designated Music Elective Programme. These programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Ahmad Ibrahim Secondary co-educational?

Yes. Ahmad Ibrahim Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Ahmad Ibrahim offer IP?

No. Ahmad Ibrahim offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### Does Ahmad Ibrahim offer the MOE Music Elective Programme?

Ahmad Ibrahim runs a school-based Enhanced Music Programme. This is distinct from the MOE-designated Music Elective Programme (MEP), which is offered at a separate set of schools. A music talent pathway may still be available through DSA-Sec — see the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder).

### What PSLE score does my child need for Ahmad Ibrahim?

Ahmad Ibrahim is competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Ahmad Ibrahim offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Ahmad Ibrahim participates in DSA-Sec across sports, performing arts, and academic talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Ahmad Ibrahim?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Ahmad Ibrahim if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
