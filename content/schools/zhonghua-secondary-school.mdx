Zhonghua Secondary School — commonly known as ZHSS — is a government autonomous co-educational school located on Serangoon Avenue 3 in the north-east of Singapore. As an autonomous school, Zhonghua has greater flexibility over curriculum enrichment and co-curricular programming than standard government schools, within MOE's national framework.

Zhonghua offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is distinctive for offering the Art Elective Programme (AEP), one of MOE's national art development pathways available at only a small number of secondary schools — Zhonghua was the first co-educational school to offer the AEP, giving it a strong visual arts identity.

## What makes Zhonghua distinctive

Zhonghua Secondary is an autonomous school with a strong visual-arts identity. Its defining programme is the Art Elective Programme (AEP), an MOE-designated pathway available at only a small number of schools — and Zhonghua was the first co-educational school to offer it, where art is studied as a formal academic subject. For DSA-Sec families, the AEP is a genuine draw for visual-arts talent and a recognised route towards art study at junior college. Beyond art, the school carries a Chinese orchestra, Chinese dance, guzheng and concert band culture and competitive basketball, table tennis and volleyball. The best fit is a child with a serious artistic portfolio or clear direction, a Chinese cultural-arts talent, or a team-sport strength. As an autonomous Express and Normal school in Serangoon, a DSA offer places students into those tracks. Confirm this year's talent areas in the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder).

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Zhonghua offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Zhonghua is a mainstream autonomous school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Zhonghua official website](https://www.zhonghuasec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Art Elective Programme (AEP)

Zhonghua offers the Art Elective Programme (AEP), an MOE-designated programme where students study art as a formal academic subject, covering studio practice and art history alongside the mainstream curriculum. The AEP is offered at only a small number of secondary schools in Singapore, and Zhonghua was the first co-educational school to run it. Selection into the AEP is through a separate MOE Art Elective Programme application; the AEP is also a recognised talent pathway and a route towards art study at the junior college level.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Zhonghua co-educational?

Yes. Zhonghua Secondary School is a co-educational school, and was the first co-educational school in Singapore to offer the Art Elective Programme.

### Does Zhonghua offer IP?

No. Zhonghua offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### How do I apply for the Art Elective Programme at Zhonghua?

The Art Elective Programme (AEP) is an MOE-designated programme with its own selection process, separate from DSA-Sec. Students apply through the MOE AEP application, which typically involves an art aptitude test and interview. Check the [Zhonghua admissions page](https://www.zhonghuasec.moe.edu.sg/admissions/) and the MOE SchoolFinder for the current AEP application details.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Zhonghua?

Zhonghua is an autonomous school competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Zhonghua offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Zhonghua participates in DSA-Sec across performing arts, sports, and leadership talent areas. The Art Elective Programme is admitted separately through the MOE AEP application. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's DSA offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Zhonghua?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Zhonghua if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
