Regent Secondary School — commonly known as RSS — is a government co-educational school located on Choa Chu Kang North 5 in the Choa Chu Kang area of north-western Singapore. The school combines the national curriculum with values-based character development and an applied-learning emphasis in environmental advocacy.

Regent offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its strong uniformed-group tradition, its band and choral culture, and an active environmental and community leadership focus.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Regent offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Regent is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Regent official website](https://www.regentsec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning and Environmental Advocacy

Regent runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) — its GREEN (Growing Responsible Ecology and Environment) Advocates programme — and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on community youth leadership. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Campus and Facilities

Regent Secondary School is located at **50 Choa Chu Kang North 5, Singapore 689621** in the Choa Chu Kang area of north-western Singapore.

**Nearest MRT:**
- **Yew Tee** (North South Line NS5) — approximately a 10-minute walk

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Regent Secondary co-educational?

Yes. Regent Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Regent offer IP?

No. Regent offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Regent?

Regent is competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Regent offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Regent participates in DSA-Sec across uniformed-group, performing arts, sports, and leadership talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Regent?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Regent if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
