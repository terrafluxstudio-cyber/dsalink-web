Seng Kang Secondary School — commonly known as SKSS — is a government co-educational school located on Compassvale Lane in the Sengkang area of north-eastern Singapore. The school combines the national curriculum with values-based character development and an applied-learning emphasis in STEM.

Seng Kang offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its hockey and team-sports tradition, its STEM focus, and an active sports and outdoor education culture.

---

## What makes Seng Kang distinctive

Seng Kang Secondary has a clear sport-and-STEM identity, known for a strong hockey tradition. Its Applied Learning Programme sits in STEM with a health and food-science focus, while its Learning for Life Programme builds character through sports and outdoor education. For DSA-Sec families, hockey is the clearest draw, together with competitive basketball, football, netball and badminton, and an applied-science strand. The best fit is a child with a sporting strength — especially hockey — a hands-on STEM interest, or an outdoor inclination, rather than a purely academic setting. Sitting in Sengkang, Seng Kang Secondary serves north-eastern families as a mainstream Express and Normal school, so a DSA offer places students into those tracks. ## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Seng Kang offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Seng Kang is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Seng Kang official website](https://www.sengkangsec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning and Outdoor Education

Seng Kang runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in STEM focused on health and food science, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on sports and outdoor education. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Seng Kang Secondary co-educational?

Yes. Seng Kang Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Seng Kang offer IP?

No. Seng Kang offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Seng Kang?

Seng Kang is competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Seng Kang offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Seng Kang participates in DSA-Sec across sports, academic, and performing arts talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Seng Kang?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Seng Kang if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
