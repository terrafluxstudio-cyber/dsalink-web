Compassvale Secondary School — commonly known as CPVS — is a government co-educational school located on Compassvale Crescent in the Sengkang area of north-eastern Singapore. The school combines the national curriculum with values-based character development and an applied-learning emphasis in STEM.

Compassvale offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its aeronautics and robotics focus, its environmental science culture, and an active team-sports tradition.

---

## What makes Compassvale distinctive

Compassvale has a distinctive aeronautics-and-engineering identity. Its Applied Learning Programme sits in STEM with a future-of-transportation focus — aeronautics, coding, engineering and sustainability — an uncommon, specific hook for a child fascinated by flight and design. Alongside that, the school carries a robotics and environmental-science culture and an active team-sports tradition. For DSA-Sec families, that draws a STEM-and-aviation strength together with competitive floorball, netball, softball and badminton. The best fit is a child with a real interest in aeronautics, coding or applied STEM, or a team-sport ability, rather than a purely academic setting. Sitting in Sengkang, Compassvale serves north-eastern families as a mainstream Express and Normal school, so a DSA offer places students into those tracks. ## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Compassvale offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Compassvale is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Compassvale official website](https://www.compassvalesec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning and Leadership

Compassvale runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in STEM focused on the future of transportation, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on community youth leadership through its Green Compass programme. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Compassvale Secondary co-educational?

Yes. Compassvale Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Compassvale offer IP?

No. Compassvale offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Compassvale?

Compassvale is competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Compassvale offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Compassvale participates in DSA-Sec across sports, academic or applied-technology, and performing arts talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Compassvale?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Compassvale if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
