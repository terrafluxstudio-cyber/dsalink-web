Bukit Batok Secondary School — commonly known as BBSS — is a government co-educational school located on Bukit Batok West Avenue 8 in western Singapore. The school combines the national curriculum with values-based character development and an applied-learning emphasis in STEM.

Bukit Batok offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its rugby and team-sports tradition, its robotics and applied-learning focus, and an active band and performing arts culture.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Bukit Batok offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Bukit Batok is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Bukit Batok official website](https://www.bukitbatoksec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning and Leadership

Bukit Batok runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in STEM focused on cities and urban landscapes, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on community youth leadership. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Campus and Facilities

Bukit Batok Secondary School is located at **50 Bukit Batok West Avenue 8, Singapore 658962** in the Bukit Batok area of western Singapore.

**Nearest MRT:**
- **Bukit Gombak** (North South Line NS3) — a short bus ride away
- **Bukit Batok** (North South Line NS2) — a short bus ride away

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Bukit Batok Secondary co-educational?

Yes. Bukit Batok Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Bukit Batok offer IP?

No. Bukit Batok offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Bukit Batok?

Bukit Batok is competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Bukit Batok offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Bukit Batok participates in DSA-Sec across sports, performing arts, and academic or STEM talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Bukit Batok?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Bukit Batok if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
