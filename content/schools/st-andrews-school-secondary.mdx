St Andrew's School (Secondary) — commonly known as St Andrew's or SASS — is a government-aided Anglican boys' school located at St Andrew's Village in Potong Pasir, central Singapore. Founded in 1862, it is one of Singapore's oldest schools, with a long Anglican mission heritage and a "family" of St Andrew's schools spanning primary, secondary, and junior college levels on a shared village campus.

St Andrew's offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; the secondary school is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its strong sporting tradition — particularly rugby, hockey, and water polo — its band, and its values-based character development in the Anglican tradition.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express, Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical)

St Andrew's offers all three mainstream secondary courses. Express students prepare for the GCE O-Level examinations over four years; Normal (Academic) and Normal (Technical) students follow the N-Level pathways with progression options. The school implements full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: the St Andrew's secondary school is a mainstream affiliated school rather than an IP school. A separate St Andrew's Junior College sits within the same village campus but is a distinct institution with its own admission. Refer to the [St Andrew's official website](https://www.standrewssec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Life-Readiness and Art Programmes

St Andrew's offers a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on community and youth leadership, and an Enhanced Art Programme as an elective. These programmes support the school's broader character and leadership development in the Anglican tradition.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Campus and Facilities

St Andrew's School (Secondary) is located at **15 Francis Thomas Drive, Singapore 359342**, within St Andrew's Village in Potong Pasir, central Singapore.

**Nearest MRT:**
- **Potong Pasir** (North East Line NE10) — approximately a 5-minute walk

## Admission Pathways

### PSLE and S1 Posting

St Andrew's admits students through the S1 posting exercise across its Express and Normal posting bands. As an affiliated school within the St Andrew's family, students from the affiliated St Andrew's primary school may receive affiliation priority in posting, subject to MOE's rules. The cut-off point (COP) differs by track and by affiliation status, and changes each year. For current and historical PSLE COP data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### DSA-Sec Admission

St Andrew's participates in DSA-Sec across sports, performing arts, visual arts, and leadership talent areas. As a boys' school with a strong rugby, hockey, water polo, football, and squash tradition alongside its band, St Andrew's DSA tends to attract applicants across these sporting and arts strengths as well as student leadership. DSA offers place students into the school's mainstream tracks.

The specific talent areas offered change from year to year. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) for the current year's full list. For key application dates, see the [DSA-Sec Timeline](/timeline). For preparation by talent area, see [DSA Interview Preparation](/dsa-interview). For historical results context, see [DSA Results](/dsa-results).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is St Andrew's only for boys?

Yes. St Andrew's School (Secondary) is a single-sex school for boys.

### Does St Andrew's offer IP?

No. The St Andrew's secondary school offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. The St Andrew's Junior College on the same village campus is a separate institution with its own admission, not an IP through-train from the secondary school.

### Does my son get priority if he comes from St Andrew's primary school?

St Andrew's secondary is affiliated to St Andrew's primary, and affiliation priority may apply in the S1 posting exercise subject to MOE's rules. For DSA-Sec, admission is governed by MOE's centralised DSA framework and talent-based selection, separate from affiliation priority. Check the [St Andrew's admissions page](https://www.standrewssec.moe.edu.sg/admissions/) for current details.

### What PSLE score does my son need for St Andrew's?

St Andrew's is competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and by affiliation status and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does St Andrew's offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. St Andrew's participates in DSA-Sec across sports, performing arts, visual arts, and leadership talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my son is not offered a DSA place at St Andrew's?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to St Andrew's if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices, with affiliation priority applying where relevant. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
