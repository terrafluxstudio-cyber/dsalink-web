Outram Secondary School — commonly known as OSS — is a government co-educational school now located at its Anchorvale campus in the Sengkang area of north-eastern Singapore. The school combines the national curriculum with values-based character development and an applied-learning emphasis in business and entrepreneurship.

Outram offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its swimming and water polo tradition, its business and entrepreneurship focus, and an active band and sports culture.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Outram offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Outram is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Outram official website](https://www.outramsec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure and campus details.

### Applied Learning and Outdoor Education

Outram runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in business and entrepreneurship, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on sports and outdoor education. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

Outram admits students through the S1 posting exercise across its Express and Normal posting bands. The cut-off point (COP) differs by track and changes each year. For current and historical PSLE COP data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Outram Secondary co-educational?

Yes. Outram Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Where is Outram Secondary School located?

Outram Secondary School is located at its Anchorvale campus in Sengkang, in north-eastern Singapore. Refer to the [official website](https://www.outramsec.moe.edu.sg/) for the latest campus address.

### Does Outram offer IP?

No. Outram offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Outram?

Outram is competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Outram offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Outram participates in DSA-Sec across sports, academic or leadership, and performing arts talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Outram?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Outram if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
