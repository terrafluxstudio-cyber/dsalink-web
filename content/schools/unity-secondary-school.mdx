Unity Secondary School is a government co-educational school located in the Woodlands area of northern Singapore. The school combines the national curriculum with values-based character development and an active team-sports culture spanning basketball, volleyball, and table tennis.

Unity offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its basketball and table tennis tradition, its volleyball culture, and a co-curricular programme that develops students through sport and applied learning.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Unity offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Unity is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Unity official website](https://www.unitysec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning and Sports Development

Unity runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in a STEM or applied-learning focus, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on sports and student development. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and character-building beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Unity Secondary co-educational?

Yes. Unity Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Unity offer IP?

No. Unity offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Unity?

Unity's COP differs by track and changes each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Unity offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Unity participates in DSA-Sec across basketball, table tennis, and volleyball. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Unity?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Unity if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
