Punggol Secondary School is a government co-educational school located in the Punggol area of north-eastern Singapore. The school combines the national curriculum with values-based character development and a programme spanning performing arts, environmental education, and team sports.

Punggol offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its choir tradition, its environmental science focus, and an active volleyball culture that anchors its co-curricular life.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Punggol offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Punggol is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Punggol official website](https://www.punggolsec.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning and Environmental Education

Punggol runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in environmental science and sustainability, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on performing arts and student development. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Punggol Secondary co-educational?

Yes. Punggol Secondary School is a co-educational school.

### Does Punggol offer IP?

No. Punggol offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Punggol?

Punggol's COP differs by track and changes each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Punggol offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Punggol participates in DSA-Sec across choir, environmental science, and volleyball. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Punggol?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Punggol if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
