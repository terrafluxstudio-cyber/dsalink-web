Paya Lebar Methodist Girls' School (Secondary) — commonly known as PLMGS(S) — is a government-aided, autonomous, Methodist school for girls located on Lorong Ah Soo in the Hougang area of north-east Singapore. As an autonomous school with a Methodist mission heritage, PLMGS combines the national curriculum with values-based character development for girls.

PLMGS offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is part of the Paya Lebar Methodist family, which includes an affiliated primary school, and is known for its strong band, harp and guzheng ensembles, and an active arts and sports culture.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

PLMGS offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: PLMGS is a mainstream autonomous school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [PLMGS official website](https://www.plmgss.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Arts and Character Programmes

PLMGS runs an in-house arts education emphasis and values-based student leadership and character development programmes alongside its academic curriculum. These are school-based signature programmes rather than MOE-designated national electives. For the current list of the school's distinctive programmes, refer to the official website.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Admission Pathways

### PSLE and S1 Posting

PLMGS admits students through the S1 posting exercise across its Express and Normal posting bands. As an affiliated school within the Paya Lebar Methodist family, students from the affiliated primary school may receive affiliation priority in posting, subject to MOE's rules. The cut-off point (COP) differs by track and by affiliation status, and changes each year. For current and historical PSLE COP data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### DSA-Sec Admission

PLMGS participates in DSA-Sec across sports, performing arts, and leadership talent areas. As a girls' school with a strong band, harp and guzheng ensembles, rhythmic gymnastics, and netball tradition, PLMGS's DSA tends to attract applicants across these strengths as well as student leadership. DSA offers place students into the school's mainstream tracks.

The specific talent areas offered change from year to year. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) for the current year's full list. For key application dates, see the [DSA-Sec Timeline](/timeline). For preparation by talent area, see [DSA Interview Preparation](/dsa-interview). For historical results context, see [DSA Results](/dsa-results).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is PLMGS only for girls?

Yes. Paya Lebar Methodist Girls' School (Secondary) is a single-sex school for girls.

### Does PLMGS offer IP?

No. PLMGS offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### Does my daughter get priority from PLMGS primary school?

PLMGS secondary is affiliated to PLMGS primary, and affiliation priority may apply in the S1 posting exercise subject to MOE's rules. For DSA-Sec, admission is governed by MOE's centralised DSA framework and talent-based selection, separate from affiliation priority. Check the [PLMGS admissions page](https://www.plmgss.moe.edu.sg/admissions/) for current details.

### What PSLE score does my daughter need for PLMGS?

PLMGS is an autonomous school competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and affiliation status and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does PLMGS offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. PLMGS participates in DSA-Sec across sports, performing arts, and leadership talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my daughter is not offered a DSA place at PLMGS?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to PLMGS if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices, with affiliation priority applying where relevant. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
