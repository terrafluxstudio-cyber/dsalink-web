Gan Eng Seng School — commonly known as GESS — is a government co-educational school located on Henderson Road in the Bukit Merah area of southern Singapore. Established by the philanthropist Gan Eng Seng, it is one of Singapore's earliest schools, and combines the national curriculum with values-based character development and an applied-learning emphasis in STEM.

Gan Eng Seng offers the mainstream Express and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding; it is not an Integrated Programme school. The school is known for its marching band and choral culture, its STEM and sustainability focus, and an active team-sports tradition.

## What makes Gan Eng Seng distinctive

Gan Eng Seng School is one of Singapore's earliest schools, established by the philanthropist Gan Eng Seng, with a long heritage and an applied-science focus. Its Applied Learning Programme sits in STEM with a sustainability focus (GESS Probes), alongside a strong marching band and choral culture. For DSA-Sec families, the draws include a marching band and choir, a STEM-and-sustainability strand, and competitive basketball, football, cross country and table tennis. The best fit is a child with a musical talent — especially marching band — a sporting strength, or a science-and-sustainability interest, who would value a school with deep historical roots. Sitting in Bukit Merah, Gan Eng Seng serves southern families as a mainstream Express and Normal school, so a DSA offer places students into those tracks. ## Programmes and Curriculum

### Express and Normal Tracks

Gan Eng Seng offers the mainstream Express (O-Level) and Normal academic tracks with full Subject-Based Banding, allowing students to take individual subjects at higher levels based on their strengths.

For P6 families: Gan Eng Seng is a mainstream school rather than an IP school. Track placement follows the S1 posting framework. Refer to the [Gan Eng Seng official website](https://www.ganengsengsch.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Applied Learning and Leadership

Gan Eng Seng runs an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in STEM with a sustainability focus, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on community youth leadership. These school programmes anchor its applied learning and student development beyond the academic curriculum.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Gan Eng Seng School co-educational?

Yes. Gan Eng Seng School is a co-educational school.

### Does Gan Eng Seng offer IP?

No. Gan Eng Seng offers the mainstream Express (O-Level), Normal (Academic), and Normal (Technical) tracks. It is not an Integrated Programme school.

### What PSLE score does my child need for Gan Eng Seng?

Gan Eng Seng is competitive for its Express and Normal posting bands, with the COP differing by track and changing each year. For current and historical data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### Does Gan Eng Seng offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Gan Eng Seng participates in DSA-Sec across performing arts, sports, and academic talent areas. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my child is not offered a DSA place at Gan Eng Seng?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Gan Eng Seng if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
