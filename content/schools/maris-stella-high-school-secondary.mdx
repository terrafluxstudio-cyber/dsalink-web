Maris Stella High School — commonly known as MSHS — is a government-aided, autonomous, SAP (Special Assistance Plan) Catholic boys' school in central-east Singapore. Founded in 1958 and run in the Marist tradition, it is one of the small number of SAP boys' schools in Singapore, offering Chinese as the first language alongside English within a values-based Catholic education.

Maris Stella offers the O-Level Express track; all students sit the GCE O-Level examinations at the end of Secondary 4. The school does not offer an Integrated Programme. Maris Stella is known for its strong Chinese language and cultural programmes, its band and Chinese orchestra traditions, and a broad co-curricular offering.

---

## Programmes and Curriculum

### O-Level Express (4-year)

Maris Stella admits students into the four-year O-Level Express track, culminating in the Singapore-Cambridge O-Level examinations. As a SAP school, Chinese is offered at a high level alongside English, with the expectation that students maintain proficiency in both languages.

For P6 families: Maris Stella does not offer an IP track. Families seeking a SAP school with an IP pathway may consider Catholic High, Dunman High, or River Valley High. Refer to the [Maris Stella official website](https://www.marisstellahigh.moe.edu.sg/) for the current academic programme structure.

### Special Assistance Plan (SAP) and Language Programmes

As a SAP school, Maris Stella offers the Language Elective Programme (Chinese) for students with strong Chinese language ability, an Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in STEM with a materials science focus, and a Learning for Life Programme (LLP) centred on sports and outdoor education.

For context on the DSA-Sec process, see [What is DSA-Sec?](/what-is-dsa).

## Campus and Facilities

Maris Stella High School's home campus is at **25 Mount Vernon Road, Singapore 368051**, near Bartley.

**Nearest MRT (Mount Vernon campus):**
- **Bartley** (Circle Line CC12) — approximately a 2-minute walk

The school has announced a temporary relocation during the redevelopment of its Mount Vernon campus. Because the campus address and nearest transport may change during this period, parents should check the [Maris Stella official website](https://www.marisstellahigh.moe.edu.sg/) for the current campus location and transport arrangements before visiting.

Campus facilities include general and specialist classrooms, science laboratories, a multi-purpose hall, performing arts and music spaces, and sports courts. For current facility details, refer to the official website.

## Admission Pathways

### PSLE and S1 Posting

Maris Stella admits students through the S1 posting exercise under the SAP category. The COP reflects the school's competitive position for the O-Level Express posting band and changes each year. For current and historical PSLE COP data, see [PSLE COP historical data](/psle-cop).

### DSA-Sec Admission

Maris Stella participates in DSA-Sec across a broad range of sports, performing arts, academic, and leadership talent areas. As a SAP boys' school with strong band, Chinese orchestra, robotics, and a wide sporting offering, Maris Stella's DSA tends to attract applicants across these strengths as well as the Chinese language pathway. ## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Maris Stella only for boys?

Yes. Maris Stella High School is a single-sex school for boys.

### Does Maris Stella offer IP or only O-Level?

Maris Stella offers only the O-Level Express track. All students sit the GCE O-Level examinations at the end of Secondary 4. Maris Stella does not offer an Integrated Programme.

### Is Maris Stella a SAP school?

Yes. Maris Stella is a SAP (Special Assistance Plan) boys' school offering Chinese as the first language alongside English, with a strong bilingual and Chinese cultural emphasis. Students are expected to have good Chinese language proficiency.

### Where is the Maris Stella campus?

The home campus is on Mount Vernon Road near Bartley MRT. The school has announced a temporary relocation during a campus redevelopment, so the address may differ during that period. Check the [Maris Stella official website](https://www.marisstellahigh.moe.edu.sg/) for the current campus location before visiting.

### Does Maris Stella offer DSA-Sec?

Yes. Maris Stella participates in DSA-Sec across sports, performing arts, academic, and leadership talent areas. All DSA offers are into the O-Level Express track. Use the [DSA-Sec School Finder](/dsa-finder) to see the current year's offerings.

### What happens if my son is not offered a DSA place at Maris Stella?

Not receiving a DSA offer does not affect the standard S1 PSLE posting process. Students can still be posted to Maris Stella if their PSLE score qualifies and they rank the school in their choices. Students who accept a DSA offer from any school are withdrawn from the S1 posting exercise entirely.
